Jamiroquai, Miguel Lead North Coast Music Fest Lineup

March 13, 2024
blog

Damian Marley performs at 2017's NCMF (React Presents)

CHICAGO, Ill. (CelebrityAccess) — Organizers for the North Coast Music Festival announced the full lineup for the event when it returns to Chicago’s Union Park on on August 31 through September 2 for its ninth edition.

Headliners for the festival are future funk pioneers Jamiroquai with one of their first U.S. performance in more than a decade and Grammy-Winning Mexican singer Miguel. Other headliners for this year’s North Coast include several EDM champs, including the duo Axwell Λ Ingrosso, formerly of Swedish House Mafia, and DJ Snake.

Other performers on the bill include Vulfpeck, Snails, RL Grime, Gramatik, DVSN, Smokepurpp, Mura Masa, Lil Xan, Cashmere Cat, Moon Taxi, Juice Wrld, The Strumbellas, Lil Skies, Robert Delong, Jacob Banks, Barclay Crenshaw, Rapsody, The Revivalists and Yellow Claw.

New for this year, the festival has also added daily showcases curated by industry collectives, featuring house and hip-hop, with Aux Cord DJS presenting on Friday, Lyrical Lemonade lined up for Saturday and Chicago’s Most Wanted on Sunday.

Single-day tickets for the festival range from $45 – $55 for GA and $65 – $75 for VIP, while a three-day pass start at $129 and range to $149 for the VIP experience, festival organizers said.
