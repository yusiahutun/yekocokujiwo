 Hello! Fashion Guest List: The summer soirées are in full swing... see the best parties throughout the month of June 

    June has been a busy month for London's VIPs who have a never ending list of summer parties on their public appearance roster. From fashion brands to sporting events, lavish sit-down lunches to luxurious fairground food, the events have been nothing short of exquisite...

    The Elephant Family's 20th Anniversary Ball

    The Animal Ball celebrated the 20th anniversary of wildlife conservation charity, the Elephant Family – established by Her Majesty The Queen’s late brother Mark Shand. Hosted by King Charles III and Queen Camilla, over 250 guests from artistic, environmental, fashion, film, political and literary worlds, descended on Lancaster House and put on their glad rags to raise money for conservation projects across Asia. They wore masks and headwear from designers including Aquazzura, Christian Louboutin, Dior, Burberry and Manolo Blahnik, and were treated to a series of musical performances from the Oxford Philharmonic Orchestra and singer-songwriter Tom Odell. The ultimate event combining fashion royalty and actual royalty, all for a good cause.

    The Serpentine Summer Party 

    VIPs from world-renowned singers to Wimbledon champions, socialites to supermodels descended on the grounds of the Serpentine Gallery in Kensington Gardens for the most sophisticated summer party on June 27. The gallery aims to raise funds to support its ongoing programs and exhibitions, by hosting an annual lavish party with live music and plenty of champagne. It always boasts the most illustrious guests who put their best fashion feet forward, and this year was no different. Venus Williams oozed chic in a black embellished midi dress and Emma Thynn channelled dopamine dressing in an orange sequinned midi with a lavish feather hem. Serpentine summer party veteran Alexa Chung opted for a white mini with fishnet tights, and Ellie Goulding made a rare appearance with her husband Caspar, and her new uncle-in-lawJay Jopling - founder of White Cube. The singer the case for leather in summer in a long sleeved top and maxi skirt two piece.

    Cartier Queen's Polo Cup

    One of the most famous polo tournaments in the world,The Cartier Queen's Cup brings the sport's finest players and the chicest of guests together every summer. The final Sunday of the competition saw VIP invitees step out in their Sunday best to celebrate the closing day. Over 120 people from fashion, the entertainment industry, stylish royals, and socialites were greeted with glasses of Cartier's own champagne before enjoying a lavish lunch.

    Veronica Beard's Summer Fair

    Celeb and royalty-adored label Veronica Beard hosted its summer party on Tuesday 20th June at the Serpentine Gallery. With what may officially be the chicest funfair in the history of funfairs, the gallery was transformed into a fairground complete with stalls and a brass band. Cuisine came in the form of fish and chips, pulled chicken sliders, and “posh dogs”, and guests played classic carnival games with the chance of winning Veronica Beard clothing, Charlotte Tilbury and Elemis products and more (dreamy). Co-founders Veronica Miele Beard and Veronica Swanson Beard flew to London to celebrate with the American brand's UK supporters.

    The V&A's Spectacular Soiree

    The Victoria and Albert Museum hosted the most incredible summer party in partnership with NET-A-PORTER to celebrate the opening of its latest exhibition 'Divas'. Of course, an event as such is only going to attract the most lavish crowd. Guests from to Lady Amelia Windsor, Olly Alexander to Emma Weymouth attended the party in South Kensington which was fuelled with lashings of champagne and plenty of cocktails. The icing on the cake was a live performance from the Sugababes who sang their iconic 2002 hit . The Diva's exhibition houses over 25 objects including costumes worn by the likes of Cher, Sir Elton John, Marilyn Monroe and Rihanna.

    Casa Raki x Candice Lake

    To celebrate designer label Casa Raki's capsule collection with photographer and model Candace Lake, an intimate drinks party was held in the quintessentially British Hyde Park Garden at The Mandarin Oriental in Knightsbridge. Tables were adorned with bright bouquets, whilst charcuterie and oysters were served alongside champagne.

    Annabel's 60th Birthday

    The iconic private members club in Berkeley Square celebrated its 60th birthday, and of course celebrated in style. It was a dazzling event hosted with Don Julio 1942 where guests were treated to special anniversary cocktails, Honey Dijon graced the DJ booth and Ricky Martin performed his top hits including What's more, members and guests were also given a customised bottle of Don Julio 1942, which featured their name and the Annabel’s logo in engraved gold letters. Talk about lavish...

    Celia B x Penelope Chilvers

    A clothing label hosting a party with a footwear brand makes the perfect recipe for the ultimate fashionable celebration. Penelope Chilvers and Celia B came together to celebrated both labels, with all guests wearing Penelope espadrilles paired with incredible Celia dresses. VIP invitees travelled from Holland Park to West Wycombe Park where they enjoyed lunch in a 17th Century temple, enjoyed cocktails and danced the afternoon away with a live band. The chicest of summer parties.
