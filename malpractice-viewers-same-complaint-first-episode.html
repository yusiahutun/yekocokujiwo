MALPTRACTICE viewers all said the same thing about the ITV drama's first episode.

The new medical drama Malpractice aired last night with some hard-to-watch scenes.

Viewers watched as senior doctor Lucinda Edwards made a series of challenging choices while working a shift.

But that wasn't the only complaint viewers had after watching the first episode.

Many moaned that they couldn't hear what was going on during the first few minutes.

Taking to Twitter, one viewer penned: "Can't hear a word they are saying, anyone else?"
