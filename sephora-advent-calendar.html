The Sephora UK Advent Calendar 2023 is on sale now.

April 08, 2024
blog

Sephora's 2022 re-launch into the UK market was met with great fanfare, as queues outside the White City store could attest. Beauty enthusiasts flocked to the endless shelves of cult-favourite brands such as Ilia, Skinfix and of course, Sephora Collection, alongside classic labels such as Dior, Chanel and Pat McGrath Labs.

Naturally, there were high hopes for the retailer's own beauty advent calendar, which debuted last year – and it didn't disappoint. But this year's offering is even more impressive, with a cohort of brands such as Drunk Elephant, Rare Beauty and Elemis all in store. If receiving 25 products in a calendar was exciting, you'll be pleased to know that this year's Sephora Favourites Calendar contains a whopping 36 products, which means certain days will reveal multiple products.

Sephora Favourites Advent Calendar 2023, £189

Sephora Favourites Advent Calendar 2023, £189

Nestled within the joyful tangerine box are sample perfume vials by Commodity and Floral Street, an editor-favourite lipstick by Rose Inc., a sumptuous, classic cleansing balm by Elemis, a Tan-Luxe self-tan serum to cheat a summer glow, and Neom's scented candle.

Boasting a £1,045 value and 23 full-sized products, this enviable ornament will have you, or someone special, fully stocked on skincare, bodycare, haircare, fragrance and make-up well into 2024.
