KCA student portal: account registration, e-learning and exam timetables

March 02, 2024
blog

KCA University is a private higher education institution based in Nairobi, Kenya. It was established in 1989 by the Institute of Certified Public Accountants of Kenya (as the Kenya College of Accountancy) to offer high-quality accountancy and financial education and training. KCA is one of the top universities in Kenya today, offering a wide range of courses, from accounting and business-related ones to arts, education, computing and IT, and others to students from Kenya as well as foreign countries.

With KCA being such a large institution, accessing student services can be quite hectic. Thankfully, the university developed the KCA student portal, where the students can access the institution's programs, academic resources, and a range of student services. Below, we have compiled all the information about the portal, from how to access it, the services it offers, and how to use it.

You need to register for an account in order to use the services offered through the KCA portal. Follow this guide to register or recover your account if you lost your password.

How to register for the KCA university portal account

The KCA university portal is only open to the current students of the university. Therefore, to prevent access of the portal by outsiders, there is no online registration of the portal account. Instead, the institute's students have to log in to the portal using a username/email address and password that is pre-given by the administration during the admission process.

Usually, the account username is the student's registration number or the provided student email address. The password, on the other hand, is the national ID or Passport, or birth certificate number. If none of these details work, you can try getting your login details from the registrar's office.

To log in to the portal and access its services, simply follow the steps below:

  • Go to the KCA university website and click on the "Student Portal" link located at the top section of the website. Alternatively, you can access the portal directly at portal.kca.ac.ke/kcaportal/.
  • In the login page that displays, enter your user ID and password and click on the "SIGN IN" button

Once you are in the portal dashboard, you can start accessing the offered student services.

Password reset for KCA portal

Forgotten your portal password? Do not worry! The student life at KCA can be quite hectic sometimes, leading you to forget your password. Luckily, the institution's administration offers an easy way to reset your password and recover access to your account. To reset the password:

  • Go to the KCA portal login page
  • Click on the "Forgot Password?" link located below the login in for. This will display an account recovery form.
  • Enter your email address (the one linked to your student portal account) and click on the "Submit Query" button. A password reset request will be sent to the administrator, and once processed, you will receive details on how to reset your portal password in your email.
  • Follow the password instructions sent to your email to set a new password and gain back access to your account.

How to use the KCA university student portal

The KCA student portal is quite easy to use, even if it is your first time. Once you are logged into the account, simply go to the dashboard and scroll through the menu. The menu items will let you access various student services that are offered at the portal, for example, your academic information and progress, financial details, etc.

What services can you access from KCA student portal?

Here is a list of services that the university offers students digitally via the student portal:

Student profile and course information

One of the main services offered at the KCA university portal is your personal details and academic information. The personal details include your name, contact information, address, phone number, etc. The details are based on the information provided during the admission process, but you can edit it in the portal to create a more accurate and up-to-date profile.

The academic information, on the other hand, includes the course you are undertaking, your current year of study and semester, your academic progress (the units you have completed, their grades, and average GPA), the units you are supposed to take each semester, among others.

E-learning

KCA university also offers an e-learning portal where students can remotely access additional learning resources to boost their academic performance. The portal is accessed through the KCA Moodle page and is only accessible to current students of the institution.

To use the portal:

  • Go to the Moodle sign up page and fill the required information to create an account. A confirmation email will be sent to your email address.
  • Follow the information provided to confirm your account and activate it
  • Go to the login in page and sign in to the moodle using your account information.

Once you are signed into the account, you can start accessing the various academic resources that are offered. These include lecture notes for various units, CATs and assignments, the KCA student library, past revision papers, and more.

Online units registration

Physical registration of the units you are taking each semester is not a fun experience. This is why the KCA student portal unit registration was introduced, allowing students to easily register their required units remotely. To register for the units:

  • Go to the student portal login page
  • Enter your login details and sign in to your account
  • Once you are logged in, go to the dashboard and click on the "academics module" and then "course registration"
  • Click on "New" to start the registration process
  • Select your academic details (school/faculty, program, learning stage, and mode of study). The learning stage is the current year and semester of study (entered as "D" for distance learning students).
  • Once you have entered all the details click on "Submit". This will redirect you to a "page actions" page where you can add the units you are taking.
  • In the page, select the current semester that you have registered for
  • Click on "add units" and then check the boxes for the units that you wish to register
  • Once you have checked all the units, click on the "select units" button. This will display a list of the units you have selected.
  • Verify that you have added all the correct units and then click on "Submit Units" buttons
  • Go back to the dashboard, then click on "academics module" > "Course registration"
  • Select the semester that you have registered for and then click on "Print Proforma Invoice" to complete the registration process.

Fees payment

KCA aims to make life as easy for students as possible so that they can concentrate fully on their academics. Therefore, instead of having to spend a lot of time trying to figure out how much tuition you are going to pay, the institution posts all the fees payment information at the student portal.

Once you are logged into your account, you can be able to access your complete fees statement, with how much fees you are required to pay for the current semester and the amount you have paid for the previous ones. The fees section also features all the baking details where you can pay for your school fees.

Exams results and transcripts

Looking to track your academic progress? The KCA university student portal helps you to do that with ease. Once the examination results are out, they are posted on the portal, thus allowing you to access your results. You can also access your transcripts and print them whenever you need them.

Exam timetable

The KCA university exam timetable is usually posted on notice boards where you can access them. However, to ensure that every student can easily access the time tables, the university also posts the exams timetable at the portal. From here, you can download it to your computer or mobile phone, thus allowing you to easily keep up to date with your exams schedule.

Academic calendar

Aside from the exam time table, the KCA portal also features the academic calendar, which lists the various dates and timeline for academic events. For example, you can be able to access the semester start dates, fees registration deadlines, unit registration deadlines, CATs dates, and exams start dates.
