Celebrity birthdays 6 April: Myleene Klass, Zach Braff and Paul Rudd

April 06, 2024
blog

Happy Birthday to these three superstar celebs! Myleene Klass, Paul Rudd and Zach Braff all have a 6 April birthday. It is no surprise that they all share the Aries zodiac sign, whose typical qualities include confidence, passion, and determination. To celebrate, we take a closer look at what they have been up to recently, and how they might be marking their big day…

Myleene Klass turns 40

You would never guess this gorgeous star is celebrating her 40th birthday! The super-fit mum-of-two has been enjoying the build-up to her big day on holiday with her daughters, Hero and Ava, in an undisclosed but very sunny location. Myleene has had a hectic start to the year, promoting MyBody By Myleene Klass, a 12-week functional training programme using a Sandbell, along with cardio boxing, ab work, Pilates and yoga - and judging by the singer's stunning physique, which she shows off to her more than 200,000 Instagram followers, it seems to be working.

Paul Rudd turns 49

Yes, one of our favourite Clueless stars is one year away from turning the big 5-0! The actor will no doubt spend the day with his wife Julie and their two children, Darby and Jack. But the star will also make room for his close friends. Just last week, he and his wife were spotted with Justin Theroux, who recently announced his separation from Jennifer Aniston.

Zach Braff turns 43

The funny star is currently on a high after debuting his new sitcom Alex, Inc, but he has other things in his mind – becoming a father. The star recently opened up to PEOPLE, revealing: "I want so many [kids]. I want like 20." The actor, who plays a dad on the new ABC series, admitted: "But I'm behind. I'm 42, so I have to find the right woman and just start making babies".
