I’m going to be real with you: Even as a fashion editor, I sometimes get stumped on what to wear. Piecing an outfit together in a new and exciting way day after day is a lot of work! That’s why I have my go-to celebrity style inspirations who spark creativity and flair, such as Meghan Markle. She’s classy and elegant no matter what she wears. So, when I found out J.Crew was having a two-day winter sale with styles up to 50 percent off, including select Markle-worn pieces, I jumped out of my seat.    

Let me rephrase: These aren’t discounted pieces inspired by Markle’s closet essentials. No, no, no — these are her exact items, including her Giselle V-neck sweater, quilted vest, and more. Best of all, half of these on-sale Markle separates are under $100, so keep reading to secure Markle’s picks for less.  

Shop Markle-Worn J.Crew Pieces on Sale

Giselle V-Neck Sweater Blazer

When I saw that the exact cozy sweater Markle wore to the 2023 Invictus Games was on sale, it was game over. Better yet, it’s 50 percent off as we speak.

Just because Markle styled a variation of this quilted vest a year ago doesn’t make it old news. Shop the 50 percent-off separate in three colors: a dark black, cocoa brown, and army green.    

Chateau Italian Stadium-Cloth Parka

Markle’s Chateau parka is the definition of elegance. But the warm material is my favorite part; it’s heavyweight without being bulky, providing peak warmth during the fall and winter months. 

Perfect Lightweight Jacket

If you’re not sure what Markle-worn piece to secure, add J.Crew’s Perfect Lightweight jacket to your cart because it’s, well, perfect. Markle has been wearing this style for years, convincing me to snag my own version last year — let me just tell you, it’s beyond worth it. With code SHOPNOW, you can take an extra 30 percent off the already marked-down coat, which features water-resistant fabric, a long hemline, practical hood, elastic bungee cord, hidden zipper, and so much more. I wear mine when it rains, or I layer it under an even heavier jacket on extra-cold days. Either way, I stay warm and dry all season long.  

But J.Crew’s winter sale doesn’t start and end with Markle. In fact, there are too many deals to count, so head over to J.Crew’s website to check out even more savings opportunities before the quick-turnaround sale is over.
