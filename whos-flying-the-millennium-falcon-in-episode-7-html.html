Whos Flying the Millennium Falcon in Force Awakens?

June 16, 2024
blog
Pew pew!

The entire promo campaign for Star Wars: The Force Awakens has been an exercise in answering questions we didn’t even know we wanted the answers to. Will Jar Jar Binks make an appearance? Will there be midi-chlorians? Is it possible to love a robot more than your own flesh and blood? And now, thanks to the new trading cards for the film, we’ve got one more: Who else besides Han Solo will be flying the Millennium Falcon in the film? If you look down at the very bottom of this Chewbacca card, the answer appears to be …

Daisy Ridley’s Rey! Congrats, Rey! We’re sure you’ll follow capably in the footsteps of Lando Calrissian and Nien Nunb.

Of course, an image like this raises more questions than it answers. Is Rey just manning the cockpit while Han’s off in a turret somewhere? Did she win the Falcon from him in a sabacc? Is Han her father, and was she persuaded by a charismatic friend to cut school and steal the ship for a day out in Chicago?

Also, if you were in need of more Force Awakens tidbits in advance of Monday’s trailer, ScreenCrush reports that the film’s runtime will be two hours and 16 minutes. Is this the single smallest amount of information you’ve ever heard about a film?
