Stella Hudgens on her fashion icons and daily wardrobe malfunctions

September 03, 2024
blog

The actress and younger sister of Vanessa Hudgens talks about her first fashion collection with Boho and all things style

    She’s already an actress, model and Instagram star… now Stella Hudgens can add ‘fashion designer’ to that list. The 21-year-old has just launched a collection with Boohoo, the affordable range inspired by her own personal style. We reckon big sister Vanessa will want to get her hands on a few of these pool party-ready swimsuits and ruffled blouses, as will Stella’s 1.2 million Instagram followers.

    Stella chatted about her go-to fashion items and wardrobe malfunctions... 

    EMBED END Image { id: "embedded408" }

    What is the one item of clothing you wear the most?A good sundress or a skirt with a crop top, especially since it’s been so hot on the West Coast recently. I’ve been living in the gingham set from my collection with Boohoo!

    If you could steal one person’s wardrobe, whose would you choose and why? Sophia Loren because she was so magical.

    What’s the best piece of fashion advice you’ve ever received? Don’t be afraid to step outside your comfort zone.

    What’s the one current trend you really can’t stand? I don’t think there’s a current trend that I really hate… I’m not the biggest fan of coloured eyebrows.

    EMBED START Image { id: "embedded409" }

    EMBED END Image { id: "embedded409" }

    Who is your ultimate style icon? David Bowie.

    What was the last item of clothing you bought? I think last thing I bought was a vintage Gucci T-shirt. I’m obsessed with Gucci. Don’t remind me how much it was.

    Have you ever had a ‘wardrobe malfunction’ in public?Oh, several. Like on a daily basis. But I just smile through it and try to change immediately.

    Do you plan your day-to-day outfits in advance or first thing in the morning? Typically, I plan my outfit last thing before I leave. I try to find whatever’s easiest, but I usually end up making a mess and taking forever.

    Do you prefer online shopping or physical stores?Online because I hate trying clothes on.

    Glamming up or dressing down? Dressing down.

    Vintage or modern? Vintage.

    Backpack or clutch bag? Clutch.

    Leather or lace? Leather.

    Mini or maxi skirt? Mini.

    Skinny jeans or boyfriend jeans? Boyfriend jeans.

    Trainers or heels? Trainers.
