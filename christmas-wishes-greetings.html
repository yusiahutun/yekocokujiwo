125 Christmas Wishes and Greetings for Holiday Cards

August 19, 2024
blog

Christmas is a magical time, but let's be honest. It can be a bit stressful. It's always important to slow down and remember why we're celebrating and then share the joy with loved ones. Let this list of Merry Christmas wishes do the work for you.

Now, if you're contemplating how to deliver your holiday message—whether card, text, or Instagram post—you'll find handy tips at the end of this list. But if your main question is what do you say in a Merry Christmas card, we’ve rounded up dozens of ideas here, ranging from heartwarming and inspirational to romantic, religious, and even funny—and all are short, so your hand doesn’t get tired writing.

Along with unique and meaningful Christmas wishes, you’ll find timeless Christmas quotes from notable figures and authors. Others hale from favorite holiday songs and beloved Christmas movies (one of our all-time favorites comes from Elf: "The best way to spread Christmas cheer is singing loud for all to hear.").

Need more ideas? To keep the reason for the season top of mind, peruse these Christmas Bible verses and religious Christmas quotes. Or, if you tend to keep your greetings on the lighthearted side, check out our collections of funny Christmas quotes and tree-mendously cute and clever Christmas puns.

Whichever path you choose, your loved ones and acquaintances are sure to appreciate the glad tidings. Merry merry to you and yours!
