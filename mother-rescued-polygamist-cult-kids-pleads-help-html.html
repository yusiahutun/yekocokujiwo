Mother of rescued polygamist cult kids pleads for help

August 22, 2024
blog

The mother of four children who were rescued from a polygamist cult compound in Utah where their father had taken them is now asking for help to look after them.

John Coltharp took his two daughters and two sons away from their mother Micha Soble in September last year to live with his friend Samuel Shaffer and his two daughters.

Shaffer and Coltharp agreed to exchange their two daughters so that they could become 'child brides' for the other man. 

The children, were aged between four and eight at the time, were all found in an abandoned compound of disused trailers near Salt Lake City in Utah.

Coltharp and Shaffer were both arrested and have since admitted charges including child rape, sodomy and kidnapping. 

The Coltharp children, who are aged between four and eight when they were taken last September, have been living with their mother for the last three months since being rescued from a polygamist cult their father had taken them to in Utah in December 

The two sisters were married off to another man in the cult for him to have sex with. They were found in an empty water barrel where he had hidden them in December. Above, they are pictured in hospital with their mother Micha Soble 

Not long before the children were found, Shaffer 'spent the night' with one of the Coltharp girls. 

The men believed that a Muslim invasion was imminent and that they could have sex with their child brides if the girls 'initiated it'. 

They formed a group called the Knights of the Crystal Blade which was based on Mormon beliefs.

The Coltharp girls were found in an empty water barrel in sub-zero temperatures where they had spent the night. They were wearing pajamas, were barefoot and required medical treatment for dehydration.

The boys were being hidden in a shipping container nearby. 

In her YouCaring page, Soble claims the ordeal has left her homeless and in desperate need of money to support the children who remain haunted by the trauma. 

By Wednesday, she had raised $2,320 out of her $10,000 target. 

John Coltharp, the children's 34-year-old father, is in custody on charges of child sodomy and kidnapping

'The children have been home for 3 months now, as long as they were missing. They are physically recovered for the most part, but emotionally, it's a far different story. 

'They struggle daily with things most children do not. 

'They do not fear imaginary monsters in the dark, they fear flesh and blood monsters who have done unspeakable acts. 

Samuel Shaffer, 32, was arrested for child rape. He is the man who Coltharp gave his daughters to 

'They fear hunger and cold, they fear not having a home. 

She was living happily with their father until he announced to her before he took them that he wanted a divorce, she said.  

'My husband at the time (John Alvin Coltharp) told me that he quit his job, was taking my children, and moving in with his parents leaving me homeless.

'Since we were still married at the time I had no way to stop him from doing so.

'I thought his plans were merely to live lazily off his parents while hurting me in the process, little did I know his plans were far more sinister than I could ever imagine,' she said. 

Coltharp took the children in September last year when he told Micha he wanted a divorce. 

It took police three months to issue an amber alert for them. Authorities said at the time that it was because she had not filed the appropriate paperwork. 

By the time they were rescued in the middle of the desert, the children had been living there for three months. 

Coltharp's parents were also there. 

'Three months my children were forced to live in freezing cold shipping containers in the desert without electricity, running water, or a non abusive adult. 

The children's mother Micha Soble said they fear 'flesh and blood monsters' and that they struggle with things daily that most children 'do not'

Micha, above with the children, said they are physically healed but are still emotionally traumatized by the ordeal 

'When the children were finally found by police in a raid, the grandparents were hiding the boys.

'The girls however were found in far worse circumstances. 2 of the girls were stuffed into a 50 gallon water barrel and the other two were locked in a rat infested trailer. 

'All 4 of them were left for over 24 hours without food or water in freezing temperatures and would not have survived night,' Soble said in her fundraising page. 

The mother said she had spent all of her money on trying to find the children and that she now needs to give them a permanent home. 

She did not say where they had been living since December but described it as 'temporary'. 

Shaffer had also taken his two daughters to live on the cult and had given them to Coltharp. They were rescued in December 

Above, one of the girls is rescued from the empty water barrell where they were being hidden in December 

This is one of the shipping containers in the Utah desert compound where the children were being held 

Coltharp is shown above in a family photograph before he joined the cult

'They need stability, they need a place to feel safe. The legal process to find them has caused me to file bankruptcy, to lose my apartment, my job, my car. 

'It's been a struggle to get them to and from therapy without a vehicle. 

'I just need to be able to get them the things they so desperately need in order to heal and recover from their incredibly traumatic kidnapping,' she said.

The Shaffer girls' mother has not yet come forward in the same way to reveal what happened to her children.

Their father recently pleaded guilty to child rape and will spent 25 years behind bars. 

Coltharp remains in custody awaiting his next court date on charges of child sodomy and kidnapping.
