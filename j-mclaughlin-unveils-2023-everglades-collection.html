J.McLaughlin Unveils 2023 Everglades Collection

April 29, 2024
blog

This spring, dress stylishly for a wonderful cause with The Everglades Collection by J.McLaughlin.

The brand is marking The Everglades Foundation’s thirtieth anniversary with the limited edition Everglades Collection and inspired print. The retailer considers Florida its second home and will be partnering with The Everglades Foundation for the sixth consecutive year. The foundation leads efforts to restore and protect the Everglades’ ecosystem through science, advocacy, and education.

The J.McLaughlin x Everglades Exclusive collection features women’s and men’s apparel and women’s accessories in the exclusive 2023 Everglades Foundation Mangrove Flower print. The print is inspired by the natural beauty of the Florida Everglades and features three endangered species: the American kestrel, Rufous hummingbird, and roseate spoonbill. The collection embraces a vacation-chic feel, transforming classic designs with vibrant colors and pulling inspiration from South Florida’s unique surrounds.

This exclusive collection is available for purchase at the Hobe Sound and Stuart boutiques, as well as through online. Fifteen percent of proceeds will be donated back to The Everglades Foundation.
