See Why Patti Stanger Says &quot;F--- You&quot; to Ex-Employees Destin &amp; Rachel

Patti Stanger isn't letting her former employees off the hook for leaving her company.

In an exclusive sneak peek of the upcoming episode of "Million Dollar Matchmaker," Stanger gets ambushed by Destin Pfaff and Rachel Federoff, who she hasn't seen since they parted ways on very bad terms.

"I have not seen these people in two or three years," Patti says. "What are you doing here?"

"We want to rekindle a friendship with you," Pfaff replies.

Stanger's former employees explain that they want to reconcile so they can all work together again. "We miss her and we want our family back," Destin declares.

This doesn't sit well with Patti, who clearly is still holding a grudge.

"They want to be friends again?" she asks the camera during a confessional. "Oh, no, no, no. I don't want to be friends with them."

"I do have something for them," she says as she pretends to reach into her jacket pocket. "I have a big fat this (as she holds up her censored middle finger)."

"What do you think we're just going to hug it out ... like this is 'Entourage?' Get over yourself. All I've ever done is be nice to you. I made you," Stanger yells. "Matchmaking is an art, that I taught. It takes years and years to master ... You need to realize what you did."
