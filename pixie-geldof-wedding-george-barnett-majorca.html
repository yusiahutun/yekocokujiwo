Pixie Geldof wedding to George Barnet in Majorca Pixie Geldof gets married in Spain

May 16, 2024
blog

Getty

Pixie Geldof tied the knot with her longtime love George Barnett in a beautiful ceremony in Majorca over the weekend. The couple – who have been together for five years – said their vows in front of family and friends including Harry Styles, Alexa Chung and Daisy Lowe, Metro has revealed.

The wedding took place in a 13th-century estate and former monastery, overlooking the Mediterranean. There are reported to have been 150 guests at the ceremony – among them Nick Grimshaw, Harry Styles, Louis Tomlinson and Thomas Cohen, who is the widow of Pixie's sister Peaches.

Alexa Chung and Daisy Lowe played bridesmaids to Pixie Geldof over the weekend. Image: Getty

Lowe and Chung are said to have been two of Geldof's several bridesmaids. They were both spotted wearing pale pink, off-the-shoulder dresses, standing beside the bride who looked beautiful in a layered tulle gown. Geldof was given away by her father, Bob.

While it looks as if there may have been a social-media ban at the wedding, with no pictures surfacing on Instagram yet, we have our fingers crossed that we'll see more snippets of the chic nuptials soon.
