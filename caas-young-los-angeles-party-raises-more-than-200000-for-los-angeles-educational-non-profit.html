CAAs Young Los Angeles Party Raises More Than $200,000 For Los Angeles Educational Non-Profit

May 27, 2024
blog

CAA’s Jennifer Okafor, Andrew Cutrow, Bella Mumma, and Spenser Noh (Courtesy CAA)

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — On Friday, Creative Artists Agency hosted its 17th annual Young Hollywood party at the Sunset Room in Los Angeles with the proceeds going to support the mission of a local Los Angeles educational non-profit.

The event was attended by more than 1,000 people, including high profile CAA agents and execs, as well as clients and friends of the agency. The list of attendees includes Chloe & Halle Bailey, Stephan James, Chad Goes Deep, Maggie Lindemann, Rickey Thompson, Jake Picking, Froy Gutierrez, JC Caylen, and Danielle Macdonald, among others.

The event supported the work of Communities In Schools of Los Angeles, an organization that focuses on preventing student from dropping out of the educational process and helped to raise more than $200,000 for the organization.

In addition to the Los Angeles event, CAA also hosted Young Parties in Nashville on October 10 and New York on October 17, which cumulatively raised $139,000 for DonorsChoose and Communities In Schools of Tennessee at Nashville.

As well, The Young Party London is slated to take place on November 13 with the proceeds pledged to support Sported UK and Nordoff Robbins.

According to CAA, the Young Parties are planned and hosted by members of the CAA Task Force, a group of young leaders who work closely with the CAA Foundation, which is dedicated to creating positive social change through strategic partnerships & campaigns, volunteerism, and works closely with the agency’s clients on their philanthropic strategies.

The Task Force was founded in 2002 and the Young Parties alone have raised more than $4.5 million over the last 17 years.
