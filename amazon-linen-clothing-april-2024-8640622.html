Breezy Linen Clothing Is Available at Amazon Starting at $20

January 29, 2024
blog

It’s starting to feel like summer, and for me, that means one thing: It’s time to pull out my linen clothes. Whether it’s a linen top, dress, or even pants, the breezy fabric keeps me cool on the hottest, most humid days. If you don’t already have a collection of airy linen pieces in your warm weather wardrobe, I highly suggest grabbing a few affordable pieces from Amazon. 

To no surprise, Amazon is my go-to place for stocking up on linen fashion for less. The retailer has tons of tops, bottoms, dresses, and even two-piece sets in the material, and prices start at just $20. (Note that the amount of linen in each piece varies.) Before it gets unbearably hot outside, give yourself the gift of comfort with these seven shopper-favorite linen pieces, below. 

Shop Linen Clothing on Amazon

Anrabess Linen Short Overalls

On sale for just $20, these Anrabess short overalls are the perfect throw-on-and-go look for summer. They’re made from a “high quality” fabric blend that contains 30 percent linen, per a reviewer, and they come in 24 colors and sizes S through XXL. Plus, the one-piece outfit has adjustable straps, so you can find your perfect fit. Throw it on with a basic tee or tank underneath, add on a pair of white sneakers, and you’re ready to head out the door. 

The Drop Nala Twist-Front Short-Sleeve Linen Mini Dress

If your wardrobe could use some color, check out this short-sleeve mini dress from The Drop that comes in bright pink, purple, and marigold (as well as classic black). As a neutral girlie myself, I understand if you want to try out the bold dress before committing to purchasing it. With Amazon’s Prime Try Before You Buy program, you can order the dress for free, try it on at home, and keep it for seven days before deciding if you want to buy or return it. Just be sure to log into your Prime account or sign up for a free 30-day trial to take advantage of this service. I have a feeling you won’t want to return the mini once you style it for spring and summer with your favorite sandals and handbag. 

The Drop Esma Linen Vest

Another fashionable find from The Drop, this linen blend vest is right on trend for the season. It comes in three neutral shades, each with a V-neckline exposed seams and three functional buttons down the front. You can dress it down with denim cutoffs and sneakers for a casual weekend day, or jazz it up with tailored trousers and ballet flats for a day at the office. And if you really want to lean into the linen look, pair the vest with the matching pull-on linen shorts from The Drop.
