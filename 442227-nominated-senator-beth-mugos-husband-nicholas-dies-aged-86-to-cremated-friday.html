Nominated Senator Beth Mugo's Husband Nicholas Dies Aged 86, To Be Cremated on Friday

August 03, 2024
blog
  • Senator Beth Mugo's husband died on the morning of Thursday, February 3, after a long battle with an illness
  • Nicholas Mugo was 86 years old when he breathed his last at the Nairobi Hospital
  • His wife of 61 years announced his death and said he will be laid to rest tomorrow, Friday, February 4

Nominated Senator Beth Mugo has been thrown into mourning after the death of her husband Nicholas Mugo at a Nairobi Hospital.

Mugo died aged 86 on the morning of Thursday, February 3, after a long battle with an undisclosed illness.

In a statement to the press, Beth said her husband will be cremated on Friday, February 4, adding the date for a memorial service will be announced later.

"With profound sadness and sorrow, I wish to announce the death of my husband, Amb. Nicholas Mugo, that happened early this morning at the Nairobi Hospital after a long illness,” she said in a statement to media houses.

Career diplomat

The deceased was a diplomat and served as Kenya's ambassador to France, Ethiopia, the Vatican and Yugoslavia among others.

He was one of the first Kenyans who benefitted from a scholarship program to advance his studies in the United States in the 1950s.

"He was among the first Africans who came back to the country to take up the responsibilities of serving the nation,” added Beth.

The ambassador was also a long-serving church elder at PCEA St. Andrew church.

Wedding anniversary

TUKO.co.ke reported when the couple became the envy of many after celebrating their 61st wedding anniversary.

This was after the couple marked the special day in pomp and colour at the Villa Lalibla in her compound in Kitusuru.

The event saw the attendance of the who is who in Kenya, among them Uhuru who was the chief guest at the event.
