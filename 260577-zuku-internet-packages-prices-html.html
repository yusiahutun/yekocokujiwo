Zuku internet packages, prices, and coverage for home and business 2022

August 28, 2024
blog

Reliable internet is vital today, whether you work at home, at the office, or are looking for connectivity for home entertainment. There are several companies offering internet services in Kenya, one of them being Zuku. Are you familiar with Zuku internet packages?

Zuku was launched in 2008 by Wananchi Group. It became known for its affordable unlimited monthly internet packages provided through a reliable fibre connection. How much does Zuku internet cost, and where can you get the service today?

Zuku internet packages and prices

What packages are offered by Zuku? Below is a look at various internet service products you can purchase from the company.

Home internet packages

The Zuku Home Fiber is one of the most popular services offered by the company. It includes internet and satellite TV packages for residential customers in various covered areas. The home Zuku WiFi packages come in two options, internet-only and triple pay option.

The internet-only option provides Zuku home fibre connection with no other added service. Zuku triple pay service offers a three-in-one package for customers – home fibre internet, satellite TV, and Zuku telephone services. The packages provided under home internet packages include:

10 Mbps package - Kshs 2,799

The package delivers a fibre internet connection with a bandwidth of 10Mbps. In addition, a Zuku router is offered at no extra, which converts the fibre connection into a WiFi network for your home use. Zuku WiFi installation is free (for first-time customers only), after which you are charged Kshs 2,799 per month.

20 Mbps package - Ksh 4,199

The package comes with a 20 Mbps high-speed fibre connection. Installation is done at no extra cost, and you are also offered a free router. The monthly price for the package is Ksh 4,199.

60 Mbps package - Ksh 5,999

You get a 60Mbps fibre connection and a free modem for this package. You also receive free installation, after which you start paying Ksh 5,999 monthly.

Other Zuku packages available on the internet-only service include:

  • Zuku internet 2Mbps price @ approximately Ksh 1,299
  • Zuku internet 5Mbps price @ approximately Ksh 2,199

Note: The Zuku WiFi prices for these packages might differ due to promotions.

While these packages are internet only, you have the option to pay extra for a satellite TV connection. Various TV packages are available, starting from Kshs 499 up to Ksh 1,149.

10 Mbps Tripe Pay - Ksh 2,899

The package comes with a 10 Mbps fibre connection and a Zuku satellite TV connection with 60 channels. Telephone service is also provided, where you get a handset (@ Ksh 1,500) and free airtime worth Ksh 1,000 (to all networks). In addition, any Zuku to Zuku calls made on the handset are free.

The package is offered without any installation costs. You are also given a free Zuku router and TV decoder. The monthly price for the bundle is Ksh 2,899.

20 Mbps Triple Pay - Ksh 4,399

With this package, you get a 20 Mbps internet connection, a TV connection with 60 channels, and a Zuku handset load with Ksh 1,000 airtime (for an extra Ksh 1,500). You also get a free Zuku router, digital TV decoder, and Zuku to Zuku calls at no charge. The bundle is available at Ksh 4,399, with free installation.

60 Mbps Triple Pay - Ksh 6,299

The 60 Mbps triple pay bundle comes with a high-speed fibre connection of 60 Mbps. You also receive satellite TV installation and a Zuku phone handset with Ksh 1,000 airtime (for an extra Ksh 1,500).

A free router and TV decoder are also offered, and there are no Zuku installation charges. The package is available at a monthly price of Ksh 6,299. Aside from these packages, Zuku also offers 100 Mbps, 200 Mbps, and 500 Mbps (the highest internet bandwidth in the country).

Business packages

Aside from home packages, Zuku also offers a business internet fibre connection for business and commercial premises. Below are the packages offered under this service:

10 Mbps Zuku Office - Ksh 4,499

This package comes with a 10 Mbps fibre connection, a basic TV package, a free WiFi router and a free telephone handset at Ksh 1,500. The monthly price for the package is Ksh 4,499.

20 Mbps Zuku Office - Ksh 5,599

The product comes with a 20 Mbps high-speed fibre connection. A free WiFi router is also offered, and a telephone set is available at an extra Ksh 1,500 (with free airtime worth Ksh 1,000). The monthly price for the package is Ksh 5,599.

50 Mbps Zuku office - Ksh 9,099

The package comes with a 50 Mbps fibre connection monthly price of Ksh 9,099. A free Zuku router and a telephone handset (for an extra Ksh 1,500) are also offered. You also get free airtime worth Ksh 1,000 for calls to any network.

80 Mbps Zuku Office - Ksh 13,099

The package comes with an 80 Mbps fibre connection monthly price of Ksh 13,099. A free Zuku router and a telephone handset (for an extra Ksh 1,500) are also offered. You also get free airtime worth Ksh 1,000 for calls to any network.

120 Mbps Zuku Office - Ksh 17,799

The 120 Mbps package comes with a 120 Mbps bandwidth fibre connection and a telephone service (available at an extra Ksh 1,500 for the handset). You also get a free Zuku router and free any network airtime worth Ksh 1,000. The monthly price for the internet connection is Ksh 17,799.

Additional decoder connections are charged an extra Ksh 500 per decoder per month. On top of that, you can request a static IP address (which does not change) for your office at an additional cost of Ksh 1,000 per month.

What is Zuku WiFi router price in Kenya?

When you purchase any Zuku packages, you are always provided with a free Zuku WiFi router and, depending on the plan, occasionally free installation. In most cases, a WiFi router costs between Ksh 2999 and Ksh 8000 in Kenya.

How much is Zuku internet monthly?

The monthly Internet cost varies according to the type of internet package ordered. In most circumstances, it ranges from Ksh 2899 for a small package like 10Mbps to Ksh 6299 for a larger package like 60Mbps.

How much is Zuku 5 Mbps?

For individuals who do not use the internet frequently, a 5 Mbps subscription is available, which is nevertheless fast and efficient. Zuku internet 5Mbps costs around Ksh 2,199.

How much is Zuku WIFI student package?

Students can get 5 Mbps for Ksh 3,999 for three months or a semester and 10 Mbps for Ksh 6,999 for the same period through Zuku. You only need to have your student identification card.
