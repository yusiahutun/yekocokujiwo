Rihanna 'aging beautifully' has loyal fans failing to recognize her

April 14, 2024
blog

Rihanna has stepped out for her latest Fenty x Puma launch, and she’s spilling all the tea, from her biggest fashion ick to her ‘future baby plans’. However, some fans think she’s looking a little different.

The music star gave birth to her youngest son, Riot Rose at the start of August, and the mom of two hasn’t stopped since. Rihanna stepped out onto her launch carpet earlier this week, with her partner ASAP Rocky, as she celebrated the new take on the iconic Fenty x Puma creepers.

Rihanna shares her biggest ‘fashion ick’ – and she’s ‘aging beautifully’

At a fashion event, of course, someone was going to ask the star what her biggest fashion ick was.

In an interview with Hype Beast, posted on TikTok, the 35-year-old hesitated, before answering: “There’s no one thing.”

“I feel like when you see something inauthentic for someone, it’s blatant, it’s blaring, I hate that. It’s forced. That turns me off.”

The Unfaithful singer isn’t one to shy away from new fashion looks, as she recently stepped out in a $400,00 watch, around her ankle. Only she could pull that off.

Riri didn’t hold back at her latest event either, standing out in a stunning purple fur coat with eyeshadow to match. However, it looked like some fans barely recognized the singer, who has been in the public eye since 2005.

“Before I read the caption. I said ‘She looks like an older Rihanna.’ Whoever did this needs to be fired,” wrote one user.

One even questioned: “Who is that?”

However, others were complimenting the star’s ‘aging’ looks: “I’m so happy she embraces herself and appears to be aging well. Never understood the need to look 23 forever.”

“ALL of us are going to age! People act like they’ll be super young forever she’s young and beautiful!!” exclaimed a fourth.”

“She’s maturing so nicely,” another wrote.

The star is hoping for a girl

After two adorable sons, it looks like the celebrity couple is hoping for a girl this time. With her fashion, makeup line, and highly successful music career, she’s done it all.

When one E! News interviewer asked if there was anything she hadn’t done, the singer jokingly responded: “So far, have daughters.”

“I’m batting at 75 percent for a boy next time. So, we’ll just keep our fingers crossed,” she continued. Before the announcement of Riot Rose, rumors were circulating that the 35-year-old had given birth to a baby girl.

However, she and ASAP quickly put those rumors to bed when they released their first adorable family photo with the two boys.
