'The Voice' Singer Kennedy Holmes Places 4th in Season 15

June 25, 2024
blog

The Voice finished off season 15 last night by crowning a new champion. Out of the final four contestants, Kennedy Holmes, Chris Kroeze, Chevel Shepherd, and Kirk Jay, it was Chevel who ultimately took home the trophy.

Although Chevel's fans—including her coach, Kelly Clarkson—were thrilled that the pint-sized singer prevailed, some were upset with the results. Many viewers thought Kennedy, who was on Team Jennifer Hudson, was robbed of victory.

The young singer, who performed her original song, "Love Is Free" during Monday night's performances, impressed the coaches from the very beginning of the show when she turned all four chairs at her Blind Audition.

A front-runner all season long, Kennedy wowed America each week with her powerhouse vocals and energetic stage presence. After her fourth place finish, some people took to Twitter to express their disappointment with the outcome.

NBC//Getty Images

"So unfair!" Kennedy Holmes should have won, hands down, no comparison," one person wrote. "@NBCTheVoice @imkennedyholmes was amazing since day one. Her voice and performances were out of this world and she deserved to win. Fourth place is a joke," one user quipped.

"I will never watch your show again because America got it wrong when they voted Kennedy Holmes in 4th place. Girl please," someone else wrote. "@imkennedyholmes you were robbed plain and simple. You were the best. PERIOD," another person added.

She might not have come out on top, but Kennedy definitely has a successful career in front of her. At just 14 years old, the artist has plenty of time to continue making a name for herself in the music industry.
