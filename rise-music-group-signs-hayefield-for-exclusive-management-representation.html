Rise Music Group Signs Hayefield for Exclusive Management Representation

September 17, 2024
blog

NASHVILLE (CelebrityAccess)—Rise Music Group, an established management firm headquartered in Nashville and Austin, TX, has announced the signing of Hayefield for exclusive management representation. The emerging country genre-bending band recently released its debut EP, Trees, which garnered attention for its fusion of country and pop-punk influences. Produced by industry veterans Austin Shawn and Adam Ernst, the EP has set the stage for Hayefield’s promising future in the music industry.

Tanner Louis, CEO and Artist Manager at Rise Music Group, expressed his excitement about the new addition to their roster, stating, “I’ve been a genuine fan of these guys for years! Hayefield’s authentic blend of country and pop-punk influences ignites an infectious energy at every show. Hayefield is going to make some noise in Music City, no doubt about that!”

Hayefield, in turn, expressed their enthusiasm for joining Rise, emphasizing the importance of finding a manager who shares their passion and work ethic. “We couldn’t be more excited to join the Rise Music Group roster. Tanner is such a solid / genuine person. We wanted someone willing to work as hard or harder than us… and I wouldn’t want anybody else having our back in this business. He’s been a fan since day one, and that’s exactly who you want supporting you along this journey. We can’t wait to share our sound and vision together.”

Over the past three years, Hayefield has performed alongside notable acts such as Austin Meade, Riley Green, Hayden Coffman, Trey Lewis, Muscadine Bloodline, and many others –  showcasing their boundary-breaking sound to audiences nationwide.
