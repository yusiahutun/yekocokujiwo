Diane Lane Brings Gorgeous Daughter Eleanor to &quot;Batman v. Superman&quot; Premiere

August 15, 2024

Like mother, like daughter!

Diane Lane made it a family affair at the New York City premiere of "Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice" on Sunday night, hitting the red carpet with her daughter, Eleanor Lambert.

The actress, 51, looked gorgeous in a yellow and green printed midi dress, black boots and dangling earrings. Meanwhile, her 21-year-old daughter showed off her killer abs in white crop top and matching pencil skirt, which she paired with nude pumps and a chic side braid.

Eleanor is Diane's only daughter with her ex-husband, Christopher Lambert.

The stunning teen, who is a senior at NYU, is a music writer at The Village Voice. She's also a Wilhelmina model.

Instagram

Lane stays private about her family life, but she recently opened up about growing older as a mother in Redbook.

"As I take each baby step toward 60 and away from, uh, 20, I'm realizing I have everything I need," she admitted. "I'm not talking about material possessions. To me, there's no greater reward than being around people you care about and can be present with. To be at home doing the crossword puzzle cheek to cheek with my daughter — that's a moment," she admitted. "Or to realize I'm the person I've always wanted to become — or, should I say, the person I always knew I was."

"I guess it's that I realize I am as smart as I thought I was. I do have some talent. I really do have conviction. I'm not a bad parent and partner, even if I make a thousand mistakes," she added. "Even when things aren't going exactly where you thought they'd go, you're getting somewhere just by dedicating yourself to what really matters. What did Helen Keller say? 'True happiness...is not attained through self-gratification but through fidelity to a worthy purpose.' I'm full of those one-liners, but I certainly believe that."
