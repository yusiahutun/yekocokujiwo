Zendaya Wears a Berluti Suit to at BAZAAR ICONS 2019

August 26, 2024
blog
  • Zendaya wore a monochrome gray Berluti suit to the BAZAAR ICONS party last night.
  • The Euphoria star has already made some head-turning appearances ahead of New York Fashion Week, wearing pieces from her latest collaboration with Tommy Hilfiger.

Zendaya knows how to make an entrance on the red carpet, whether she's dressed as a fashionable Joan of Arc cosplayer or a glow-in-the-dark Cinderella, and last night at the BAZAAR ICONS party was no different. The Euphoria star arrived to The Plaza hotel looking effortlessly cool in a gray printed suit by Berluti.

Jamie McCarthy//Getty Images

From the menswear brand's Winter 2019 collection, the monochrome look consisted of a double-breasted jacket over a matching shirt, skinny tie, and trousers. The young fashion icon wore black Christian Louboutin pumps with tights to top off the ensemble.

Dimitrios Kambouris//Getty Images

Laurent Viteur//Getty Images

Zendaya has already been on quite the style streak in the past few days ahead of fashion week. On Thursday night, she wore a red velvet look from her TommyxZendaya collaboration with Tommy Hilfiger at The Daily Front Row's Fashion Media Awards.

Brian Ach//Getty Images

The night before, she looked chic in a black and white polka dot midi dress from the same collection. After previewing a few pieces, the Spider-Man: Far From Home star will debut the full range on Sunday with a runway show. If it's anything like last season's disco-themed extravaganza, the upcoming show is sure to be a blast.

Jamie McCarthy//Getty Images

Zendaya previously talked BAZAAR.com about how she considered herself "a strong influence for young people" through her personal style—and we agree.

"I like to think of myself as a strong influence for young people, and I think a big thing about being young is finding out who you are, experimenting, not being afraid to try things, and not being afraid to really embrace who you are and what makes you happy. And I think fashion is a big part of that. You don't have to care about what people think or let people determine your happiness. Do whatever the heck you want."

Preach.
