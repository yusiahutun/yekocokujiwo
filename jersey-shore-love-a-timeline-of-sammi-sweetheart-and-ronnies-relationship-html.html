A Timeline of Sammi Sweetheart and Ronnie's Relationship

July 10, 2024
blog

Prince Harry and Meghan Markle, Sandy and Danny from Grease, Cinderella and Prince Charming, these are the couples that stand the test of time. But none of them had more of an impact on this generation than Sammi “Sweetheart” Giancola and Ronnie Ortiz-Magro from Jersey Shore.

The couple had one of the most tumultuous relationships in reality television history. And while Giancola may be engaged to boyfriend Christian Biscardi, she’ll always be Ortiz-Magro’s girl in our hearts.

Let’s take a look back at the rocky relationship.

It all started back in the summer of 2009 when Jersey Shore first aired. On the first night, Giancola had to make a choice between Ortiz-Magro and Mike “The Situation” Sorrentino. She ultimately chose the former and the two began their summer fling.

The couple ended the season together, but their relationship wasn’t in the clear yet. During the season one live reunion, the two broke up after footage was aired of a conversation between Sorrentino and Giancola.

In April of 2010, the two were reunited for season two of the show. The pair claimed that they had not spoken since the reunion. At the beginning of the season, Ortiz-Magro went out and hooked up with a few girls, only to return home and sleep with Giancola.

Seeing this, Jenni “JWoww” Farley and Nicole “Snooki” Polizzi wrote the Jersey Shore sweetheart an annonymous letter detailing all of the things that her boyfriend did. Despite receiving the letter, Giancola stayed with the guido for a little while longer.

In season three of the show, the couple became even more volatile. They fought constantly and Ortiz-Magro eventually ended up destroying Giancola’s belongings, causing her to break up with him and leave the house. She did return to the show two episodes later, though.

The two toyed with the idea of getting back together throughout the rest of the season but eventually decided to stay apart when Giancola admitted that she hooked up with The Situation’s friend once before she and Ortiz-Magro were together.

In 2011, the fourth season of the show took the cast to Italy. If you thought that the pair’s love drama wouldn’t cross country borders, you were wrong. The couple continued to break up and make up throughout the season.

The next year, was the last for Jersey Shore. At the live reunion, the pair said that they were still together and were moving in together.

Their happiness didn’t last long. By 2014, the pair decided to go their separate ways.

“At this time, Ronnie and I have gone our separate ways,” Giancola told E News.” I wish him nothing but the best. Right now, I’m focused on myself.”

“We were drifting apart and not really spending any time together,” her former beau told People at the time. “I still care about her. She’s a great girl, but we were growing distant. It was for the best.”

On the Jersey Shore Family Vacation premiere, Ortiz Magro clarified why they broke up.

“Once we left the shore house, me and Sam decided we were going to take the next step,” he said. “And we were getting towards that stage where it was like, I either have to s— or get off the pot.”

“She gave me a timeline. She was like, I want to get married in six months—and I’m still trying to figure my life out,” he explained. “I got cold feet. I wanted to do the right thing, but there was a part of me that was like, ‘I’m not going to do the right thing.’ So I didn’t do it. I ended up cheating, she found out. I blew it.”

The pair tried to make it work again for a short time in 2016 before moving on to different partners.

In 2017, Giancola began dating her now fiance, Biscardi.
