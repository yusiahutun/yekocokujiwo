Naked cleaners expose 'seedy' truth behind industry: 'People ask for sex'

February 13, 2024
blog

Naked cleaners have spoken out about the sleazy side of the industry.

While the women are paid to tidy up in the buff, some said that they felt “pressured” into providing extras.

Even though companies warn clients not to ask for intimate favours, adverts have emerged online asking the workers to allow “touching” and “sexual relief”.

A Metro investigation spoke to naked cleaners about their experiences – as well as the men who hire them out.

One website that offers up the saucy service lays out strict ground rules for customers.

One of their statements read: "Our ladies are not escorts and will be very offended if you ask”.

Despite this, women have alleged that this regulation isn’t always stuck to.

The investigation claimed that this company offers £75-an-hour “Nude Plus” sessions.

It added that an undercover reporter was told that this may entail masturbating clients, as well as herself.

Online adverts appeared to echo this, encouraging women to strip off before providing their customers with “sexual relief”.

The report alleged that a customer was seen to be looking for an “attractive and educated” woman.

He clarified: “I am not looking for sex, but *some touching* – details to be discussed.”

The newspaper reports that men who pay for naked cleaning have admitted to asking for extras.

An anonymous man, who said he paid nude women up to £80 a time, admitted: “The purpose of them being there nude is for me to watch.”

He added that he pleasures himself and will “sometimes take it further”.

This doesn’t seem to be an isolated incident, as domestic workers have also suggested that the job is seedier than you may expect.

One cleaner, Anna, said that she regularly has to remind clients not to touch her.

She claimed: “I get a lot of people asking for sex which can be really frustrating when you’ve specifically said you’re not offering sex.

“I tell them to get their hands off me. They act stupid, like, ‘oh, I forgot,’ or, ‘sorry, you’re so pretty’.”

Another worker, who decided to remain anonymous, shared a similar story.

She revealed: “People sometimes ask for sex, and I repeat that I am a stripper, not a prostitute…

“I don’t have to touch them, I don’t have to watch them, just a guy is doing that while I clean.”

Previously, a naked cleaner spoke to Daily Star Online about her experiences.

While she was more open to intimate extras, she confessed that she had received some very bizarre requests in her time.

She said: “It’s strictly viewing but I would [have sex] if I fancied them. I don’t mind if they want to masturbate but I won’t let them touch me.

“I did date one of the clients for a while but he didn’t like the idea of me cleaning for anyone else so it didn’t last long.”
