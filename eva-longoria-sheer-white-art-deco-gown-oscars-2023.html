Eva Longoria Is Utterly Angelic in a Sheer White Art Deco Gown at the 2023 Oscars

February 14, 2024
blog

Eva Longoria looked angelic while walking the carpet tonight at the 2023 Oscars.

The actress was a vision in all white, modeling a semi-sheer gown from Zuhair Murad's spring/summer 2023 couture collection.

The monochrome dress was a work of art in itself—featuring geometric patterns throughout and embellished with clear crystals and white pearls. It featured a plunging neckline and built-in choker strap that doubled as a shimmering necklace. The sleeves were breezy though fastened at the wrists, and the Art Deco–inspired design came together at the center of the skirt, which cascaded down to the carpet.

She was one of several celebrities who opted for sheer white designs tonight.

Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

Longoria continued the diamond motif in her accessories: diamond rings and diamond flower-shaped cluster earrings by Chopard. She opted for a relaxed, gelled-back wavy 'do and shimmery glam, including a sparkly gray eye shadow, dramatic black lashes, a glossy pink nude lip, and a glossy white manicure resembling Hailey Bieber's viral glazed donut manicure.

On her Instagram Story, she shared various videos of her makeup and hair teams getting her ready for the glamorous awards show.

Mike Coppola//Getty Images

The Desperate Housewives star is not nominated for an award at tonight's 95th annual Academy Awards, but her attendance at Hollywood events never disappoints. The stylish actress regularly shows off her impeccable style at industry events, during press tours, on the streets, and at fashion shows. For example, she is a regular at designer friend Victoria Beckham's runway shows and is often photographed in looks from her chic brand.
