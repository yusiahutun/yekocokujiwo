60 Best Hair Products of 2024

January 28, 2024
blog

Bouncy blowouts, slick buns, and curl hacks have been everywhere lately—and for good reason. “We spent the last few years really focused on hair and scalp health,” says celebrity hairstylist Justine Marjan. “But women are ready for higher-maintenance styling routines.” Today’s trending hair looks bridge the gap between healthy and stylized hair. “The products of today can block out frizz and give strands mirrorlike shine, but without weighing down the hair or compromising hair quality,” says celebrity hairstylist Chris Appleton. Here, we’ve gathered the innovative products and tools that make styling a breeze, all while nourishing strands along the way.

Armando Grillo - Gianluca Carraro

The bouncy blowout trend is still going strong, as seen at Retrofête's fall 2024 show

Irons and wands get a lot of credit, but it’s styling products that take your hair looks to the next level. “I personally could not do my job without hairspray,” Marjan says. “It is so versatile and can be used in a variety of ways to give the hair hold and texture.” To keep hair healthy, one of the most important stylers you should never go without is a heat protectant. “If you go in the sun and don’t put sunscreen on, you’ll burn. The same goes for your hair,” Appleton adds.
