Taylor Swift's hidden message to Travis Kelce at concert that no one noticed

June 22, 2024
blog

Fans have failed to notice a sweet gesture by Taylor Swift for her beau Travis Kelce during a concert on her Eras Tour in Argentina.

While Taylor Swift was performing in Buenos Aires earlier this month, she performed a small tribute to the NFL player. Travis Kelce was seen at one of her concerts in Argentina. It could be argued that Taylor’s hold on him lasts thousands of miles, as he jet-setted from America to support the singer.

Taylor Swift’s sweet gesture to Travis during her concert

Swifties who attend the Eras Tour will know that during her concerts, the audience wears bracelets that light up different colors. But as the music star sang her song, Is it Over Now? from the vault tracks on her version of 1989, the colors changed to red and white.

These are the colors of the Kanas Chiefs, the American football team that Travis Kelce plays for. How sweet!

It comes after the celebrity donned the same colors to support the athlete at the first game she attended back in September. The internet went wild when they saw her cheering him on from his private box.

Bracelets are their thing

This isn’t the first time that the celebrity couple has been seen with the jewelry, which has become somewhat of a symbol for them.

Taylor Swift wore an ’87’ bracelet when she attended a Chiefs game on October 22 as subtle support for her celebrity boyfriend. The 87 beads on the accessory are surrounded on both sides by beads that feature pink love hearts.

Travis has previously said on the podcast that he shares with his brother, New Heights, that he tried to give Swift a friendship bracelet with his number on it after one of her concerts. Back in the summer, the hopeful had watched her sing and hoped they could meet afterward.

He explained: “If you’re up on Taylor Swift concerts, there are friendship bracelets and I had received a bunch of them, but I wanted to give Taylor Swift with my number on it.”

The 33-year-old said that he took it “personally” that she wouldn’t meet him after the show, though he did acknowledge that she tends not to talk after shows.

“I was disappointed that she doesn’t talk before or after her shows because she has to save her voice for the 44 songs she sings,” he said.

We now know they managed to meet up somehow, with the star revealing how Travis and Taylor were brought together in a recent interview. Cupid definitely had a part to play.
