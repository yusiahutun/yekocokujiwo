Zendaya unveils her longest hair length yet for Paris Fashion Week

August 17, 2024
blog

Zendaya has retired her go-to bob, debuting a set of hip-grazing brunette extensions for an appearance at Paris Fashion Week.

The actor and beauty chameleon attended Louis Vuitton's spring/summer 2024 show this weekend at 103 Avenue des Champs-Élysées – the site of the fashion house's new Paris store –stepping out with what is likely her longest hair length yet.

Marc Piasecki//Getty Images

She was also seen showcasing the super-long lengths while out in Paris the following day, while wearing a more casual ensemble of jeans and a vintage Louis Vuitton top.

Jacopo Raule//Getty Images

There is no denying that short hair has reigned supreme this year. From committing to buzzcuts to trialling statement wigs, Hailey Bieber, Emily Ratajkowski and Florence Pugh have all experimented with short and striking cuts for 2023. And Zendaya herself participated in the trend, sporting a classic shoulder-brushing bob for numerous red-carpet event before finally retiring her chic crop this weekend. Does this sudden beeline to back to long hair mark end of the 'boy bob' era?

The Euphoria star also showcased extra long locks for the Louis Vuitton Menswear show back in June, opting for a softly textured curl over the sleek, straight finish of her latest Paris Fashion Week look (see below). This time, her glossy, flat-ironed look was tucked neatly behind her ears and finished with a tidy centre part and a healthy mirror-like shine.

Zendaya completed the look with a Louis Vuitton white zip-up gown with padlock detailing – custom-made, naturally – small hoop earrings and a monochromatic palette of peach-toned make-up; think glossy coral lips, dewy cheeks and a generous haze of warming bronzer. If you're looking for a lesson in transitionally seasonal make-up, look no further than this flattering hue.
