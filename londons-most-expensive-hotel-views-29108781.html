The Lanesborough charges up to a massive £27,000 per night, reports MyLondon.

It’s known for providing incredible service, luxury finishes and even personal cars for your use.

Located near Hyde Park Corner in Knightsbridge, the exclusive Royal Suite at the Lanesborough spares absolutely no expense – and has a price tag to match.

Among the many amenities on offer the luxurious suite has a 24 hour Butler service, as well as space for up to 17 guests.

The pricy suite also comes with a chauffeur-driven Rolls Royce Phantom worth £320,000 for your journeys around London while you stay.

The suite has seven bedrooms and seven bathrooms – so you’ll never have to wait to use the lav.

Plus, each room has its own unique design and they all ooze regency charm and extreme luxury to make you feel like an old world Princess.

Thanks to its central location steps from Buckingham Palace and opposite the late Queen's former home of 145 Piccadilly you’ll feel like royalty from the moment you wake up.

This also means that the views from the Royal Suite are similar to those enjoyed by King Charles as they look out over the Palace's famous gardens.

The room has two drawing rooms and a dining room that can seat twelve.

Antique 18th-century oil paintings sit on the walls and shimmering chandeliers hang from the ceilings.

We're sure you’re as gobsmacked as we are at the decadence displayed (and wishing you could spend the night!).
