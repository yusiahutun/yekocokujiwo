Using brown kraft paper as a Christmas tablecloth is a festive hosting trend we can get behind

July 06, 2024
blog

Hosting season is well and truly upon us, meaning picky bits and festive spreads aplenty. A recent trend blowing up on social media proves that some of the simplest ways to serve food can also look the most effective – and you might already have what you need at home...

If you love using brown kraft-style paper to wrap your presents, consider setting some aside for your next festive gathering and using it as customisable tablecloth.

If you’re creating a charcuterie board/grazing table-style affair, you can spread dishes onto the paper and write/draw directly onto the tablecloth to signpost what each thing is. You’re essentially turning your table into one giant cheeseboard.

Grape & Fig, the UK’s first grazing table company love doing this for a dinner party, and have shared their top tips on Tik Tok to ensure it goes smoothly:

  • Parchment paper works just as well as kraft brown paper
  • Place cheeses onto mini dishes to protect your surfaces from cheese knives
  • Label glasses and plates too to make the tablescape extra fun
  • Serve a variety of wines to pair with different cheeses.

This idea works for a sit-down dinner, too. Lay the same parchment paper or brown paper as you would a table cloth and, instead of name tags, draw people’s names directly onto it. Then, give each guest some pens and let them have fun doodling or drawing.

In between courses or after you've eaten, the table can then turn into a creative free for all, perfect if you’re incorporating quizzes and games into your evening.
