The Lincoln Project

The Lincoln Project is an American political action committee that was formed in 2019 by former and incumbent Republicans. The main aim of the project was to prevent the re-election of Donald Trump in 2019's elections and to defeat all Republicans in close races that were running for re-election in the United States senate.

The Lincoln Project founders were Rick Wilson, Steve Schmidt, George T. Conway III, Reed Galen, John Weaver and Jennifer Horn.
