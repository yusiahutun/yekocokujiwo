Max Verstappen's girlfriend Kelly Piquet poses nude in racy photoshoot

Kelly Piquet is known in F1 circles as the other half of Max Verstappen - as well as being the daughter of three-time world champion Nelson Piquet.

She was at the centre of the celebrations at the weekend as Verstappen sealed his second consecutive F1 title by winning the Japanese Grand Prix, albeit in rather farcical circumstances.

However, the Brazilian has a successful career in her own right as a model and recently she posed for a racy shoot with Mirror Mirror magazine, wearing expensive Cartier jewellery.

They followed a post which shows her kissing her fella, who was clutching the Japanese Grand Prix winners’ trophy, on the cheek following Sunday’s race.
