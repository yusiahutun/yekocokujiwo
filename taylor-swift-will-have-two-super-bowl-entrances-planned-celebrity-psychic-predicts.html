Taylor Swift 'will have two Super Bowl entrances' planned, celebrity psychic predicts

May 16, 2024
blog

Exclusive: Celebrity Tidbit sat down with a psychic medium who predicted what’s to come in tonight’s highly anticipated Super Bowl Game, including Taylor Swift’s grand entrance to support her boyfriend, Travis Kelce.

Tonight will see Travis Kelce and the Kansas City Chiefs take on the San Francisco 49ers for that all-important Super Bowl title. Should they win, it’ll mark the NFL team’s fourth title. All eyes will also be on Taylor Swift, and here’s what celeb psychic Adam Norton predicts about her highly anticipated entrance…

Taylor Swift will have a ‘route plan’ for her Super Bowl entrance, psychic predicts

When asked if he thought everyone’s favorite celebrity couple would face any challenges leading up to the game, Adam said: “I don’t see the stress of the build-up of the game causing any tensions or issues.” Good to know!

However, he does predict an extremely thought-out route plan for the star’s grand entrance: “They will have a route plan for her entertaining the stadium. There’s going to be other people thinking ahead about her entrance,” he tells Celebrity Tidbit.

“There’s going to be two entrances. One entrance prior, i.e. people planning an entrance, and the second being her entrance.”

“But I see a lot of smiles around, a lot of happiness. I don’t see any stress or tension,” he concludes.

Adam also predicted that Taylor’s intention had always been to go to the game after her Tokyo appearances, despite some fans fearing she might not make it.

“I do feel that that has been her intention all along, I don’t really feel there’s been any questions in terms of ‘will I go, won’t I go?'” he says.

All that worry for nothing!

The star has been seen on a plane from Tokyo

After the music star wrapped up her shows in Tokyo, where she divulged info on her new album, she was seen taking a jet to head back off to the States.

At her final show at the Tokyo Dome on Saturday, the star announced to her fans that they were all gonna go on a great adventure,” which fans suspected to be about her rush back for the huge NFL game.

The big game kicks off at 3.30 pm Vegas local time, so the time difference fully worked in Taylor’s favor. Now, she can hope luck stays on her, and Travis’ side!
