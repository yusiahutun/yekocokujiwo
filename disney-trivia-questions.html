150 Disney Trivia Questions and Answers That Will Win Game Night

March 04, 2024
blog

There are Disney fans, and then there are Disney super-fans, people who've seen all the movies and Disney+ television shows, been to every single park, gone on all the cruises, and have a houseful of mouse merch (like these seriously snazzy loungefly bags). But no matter the level of your obsession, you're sure to be as happy as Tigger on a bouncing binge with the following mega-list of carefully curated Disney trivia questions.

They're perfect for family game night, because we've included plenty of easier queries great for littles, as well as a couple of toughies that just might stump mom and pop. They'll test your knowledge of everything Disney, from company history to classic quotes, beloved songs to favorite characters and, of course, Mickey and Minnie's dear old dad, Mr. Walt Disney.

So slide on your Mouseketeer ears...er, thinking cap...and see how many of our 150 questions you can answer correctly. (Hint: you're going to want to brush up on topics like Disney princesses and villains, and count on getting quizzed about newer series and older movies, too.) We can't say that you'll win any prizes for getting all of them right, unless you want to reward yourself with one of these adorbs advent calendars. Hey, we sure wouldn't blame you!
