“So I like to share my story, which is basically that I started from 35. I started from scratch… in all my 20s I was just working and being a teacher was kind of like being in a nunnery and giving up your life for God.”

But, after suddenly being confronted with a difficulty in her personal life at 35, Louise’s friend took her to the gym and subsequently kickstarted her fitness journey.

“I just found my release by thrashing it in the gym, so at 35 it took me six months to lose half my body fat and gain a lot of muscle,” she recalled.

Five years later and entering her forties, Louise has turned her passion for fitness into a business by sharing racy content including workout videos on OnlyFans.

“All of a sudden I’m 40, my Instagram has blown up, I’m about to fly out to Miami, LA, and Vegas to do photoshoots and collaborations… it’s just crazy,” she explained.

“Fitness has saved my life, without sounding too dramatic.”

She continued: “But also, I would not be earning what I am earning now on OnlyFans had I not transformed my body and got into this shape and grown my Instagram.

“I started in January 2021, and my gross net on OnlyFans at the moment is nearly half a million, yet in January 2021, a few weeks before that I was at the foodbank."
