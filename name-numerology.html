How Your Given Moniker Affects Your Personality

September 22, 2024
blog

It may sound too good to be true, but name numerology can help you discover the ways your name has been influencing you since birth. From the standpoint of numerology, the name a person is given when they enter the world is the exact name they were meant to have. The parent or person naming the child is connecting with the child’s natural vibrations prior to birth and chooses a name that best aligns with their future personality. So, the name that is on your birth certificate—even if it was changed later for any reason—is the name to use when looking up your name numerology.

What do letters have to do with numbers, anyway? Every letter from A to Z is associated with a corresponding number 1 to 9. Using these numbers, a numerologist can calculate some of the most important numbers found in people’s numerology charts, like Expression numbers, Personality numbers, and Soul Urge numbers. These core numbers are all derived directly from your full name at birth and can tell you a lot about your strengths and skills, your outward persona, and your spiritual purpose.

There’s more to your name than just adding up the numbers. Since every letter of the alphabet has its own meaning, that means every letter of your full name has a unique meaning. Additionally, your Big Three are the three individual letters of significance in your name’s numerology that can reveal even more insights into your personality. Keep reading to learn about these special letters, then discover the meaning of every letter A to Z.

1. The Cornerstone

The very first letter of your first name is known as the “Cornerstone” in Numerology and it represents your approach to life. It reveals your overall outlook and the ways you react to both opportunities and upsets.

2. The Capstone

The last letter of your first name is called the “Capstone” and it indicates how you find closure and follow through on projects and commitments. Together, the Cornerstone and Capstone show your ability to both embark on and accomplish your goals.

3. The First Vowel

The first vowel in your name doesn’t have a special title but it does have special significance. It represents the essence of your soul and what you hold most dearly. It’s a part of you that you wear on the inside and that most others will never see. The first vowel reveals the real, personal reason behind all your motivations.

A

The A represents confidence, independence, and proactivity. As part of a name, it influences people with both leadership and motivation.

B

As the second letter of the alphabet, B relates to balance and instinct. It introduces an influence of friendliness and cooperation to a person’s name Numerology.

Creative and communicative energies are carried in by C. As part of a name, it can make a person charming, inspirational, and expressive.

D

E

F

G

H

I

Tolerance and compassion are introduced by an I in a person’s name. Its presence makes them altruistic, creative, and kind.

J

J is self-sufficient, driven, and full of potential. People with J in their name are skilled at creating opportunity and carving their own path.

K

A powerful force of intuition is associated with K. It influences a person with strong instincts and an ability to work well with others.

L

There’s a friendly presence to people with L in their name. They are influenced by magnetic, optimistic, and expressive energies.

M

N

O

P

Z
