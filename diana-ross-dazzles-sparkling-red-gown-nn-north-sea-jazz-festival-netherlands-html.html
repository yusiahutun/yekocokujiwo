American soul singer Diana Ross put on a mesmerizing performance on Thursday night at the 2022 North Sea Jazz Festival, held in Rotterdam, in a sparkling red sequined gown with dramatic tulle sleeves and matching lipstick.

While on stage, the 78-year-old singing sensational, who became Motown's most successful act during the 1960s, radiated beauty as she took down the house while belting some of her biggest hits over nearly six-decade-long career. 

As she captivated the audience, the Endless Love singer shimmed on stage while holding a scarlet red bamboo fan before running her fingers through her beautiful afro.
