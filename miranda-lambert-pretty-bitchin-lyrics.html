Here Are the Lyrics to Miranda Lambert's 'Pretty Bitchin''

June 17, 2024
blog

Miranda Lambert is celebrating her happy life in her new song, "Pretty Bitchin'," and the lyrics are an unapologetic ode to how good she really has it.

Lambert dropped "Pretty Bitchin'" at midnight on Wednesday (Sept. 4), and the track pays homage to a contented life.

“‘Pretty Bitchin’’ is so much fun. Everything in it explains me … it talks about airstreams, Tito’s, having a band and awesome fans,” Lambert says in a press release. “It’s a good thank you to life for being amazing and for getting to live this dream. It’s definitely pretty bitchin’.”

While the lyrics to "Pretty Bitchin'" paint a picture of a very happy life, Lambert also takes the opportunity to poke fun at herself and her public image in the third verse, singing, "Well, I'm a pretty hot mess / But hell, I guess / I'm pretty sure it's a family tradition / I got a pretty good time in the checkout line / With all the free press I've been gettin' / It's pretty bitchin' / It's pretty bitchin'."

"Pretty Bitchin'" will appear on Lambert's forthcoming album, Wildcard. The project is set for release on Nov. 1.
