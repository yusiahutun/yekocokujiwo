Blinks and Swifties unite! Blackpink’s Thai rapper and dancer extraordinaire turned up in Singapore for K-pop group Shinee’s concert first before popping over to the National Stadium the next evening to see Tay Tay.

She isn’t the only Swiftie in the group, either – bandmate Rosé previously posted a TikTok of herself singing along to Swift’s greatest hits at her Eras Tour stop in Tokyo.

Lisa wasn’t in the Lion City for long though, departing shortly after the show to attend Louis Vuitton’s autumn/winter 2024 show at Paris Fashion Week. A busy one she is …

2. Sorn – night two

Looks like Lisa is far from the only K-pop star who is also a Swiftie. Chonnasorn Sajakul, better known as Sorn, is another K-pop idol who also happens to hail from Thailand, linking up with Lisa in the crowds on night two to sing and dance the night away.

3. Choi Min-ho – night two

Shinee’s own concert in the Lion City didn’t stop band member Choi Min-ho, aka Minho, from going to the Eras Tour. While he didn’t appear in any photos with Miss Americana herself, he posted several Instagram Stories of Swift from night two of the concert, as well as footage of himself jamming out to her songs.

4. Shou Zi Chew – night two

TikTok CEO Shou Zi Chew has become somewhat of a local celebrity for, erm, asserting his Singaporean identity under intense questioning by American congressmen who insist he might have connections to the Chinese government. Chew was spotted attending the second evening of Taylor’s Eras Tour concerts in the city state.

5. Barry Keoghan – nights one, two and three

Irish actor Barry Keoghan of Saltburn fame, who first sparked dating rumours with Swift’s opening act, Sabrina Carpenter, back in December 2023, turned up on all three opening nights of the Singapore concerts to support his new boo. Aside from taking a selfie with a lucky fan, Keoghan was also spotted sharing a sweet hug with Carpenter after her set.

6. Carina Lau – night four

Hong Kong’s very own beloved veteran actress Carina Lau stopped by the Eras Tour on March 7. Lau previously shared an Instagram Story of her tour merch, concert tickets and a welcome bouquet from Marina Bay Sands. Talk about the VIP treatment.

7. Travis Kelce – nights five and six

By now, most Swifties should be familiar with her and Kansas City Chiefs star Travis Kelce’s budding love story. They might also be familiar with the many, many miles Swift and Kelce have travelled to see and support each other at their various engagements. Swift previously flew straight from her Tokyo Eras Tour stop to Las Vegas to cheer Kelce on at this year’s Super Bowl.

Now, it seems, Kelce has returned the favour. After supporting Swift previously at her concerts in Sydney, the American football star dashed back to the US for his brother Jason Kelce of the Philadelphia Eagles as he announced his retirement. That didn’t stop him from flying straight back to this side of the world though, to attend the final two nights of Swift’s Asia concerts as well as spend some quality time with his new girlfriend in Singapore. Karma is the guy on the Chiefs, indeed.

8. Jasmine Yen – night six

Singer-songwriter Jasmine Yen, daughter of legendary actor Donnie Yen and beauty queen Cissy Wang, also took time out of her busy schedule to stop by the Eras Tour, posting a series of Instagram Stories from the show. Yen is currently promoting her latest single, “Forever 19”.

9. Heart Evangelista – night six

Filipina fashionista, actress and social media star Heart Evangelista stopped by Swift’s final Eras Tour concert in Singapore. Ever the sweetheart, Evangelista posted a series of Instagram Stories about her experience and took selfies with fellow adoring fans.
