Rose comes from the disaster-plagued Kennedy clan. Her grandparents were  President John Kennedy, who was assassinated in 1963 and Former First Lady Jacqueline who died aged just 64 in 1994.

They had three children, John Jr, who was killed in a 1999 plane crash, Patrick who died as an infant and Caroline, Rose's mother who married exhibit designer Edwin Schlossberg and is now US Ambassador to Australia.

Rose was raised with siblings Tatiana, now 32, and Jack, 30, on the Upper East Side of Manhattan.

She was just five when her beloved grandmother passed away from Non-Hodgkin's Lymphoma in 1994.

She attended the prestigious Brearley private school, where tuition is $60k a year.

After high school, she went on to get an English degree from Harvard University.

While she later dabbled in politics, filmmaking and fashion, Rose has settled into a relaxing life on the West Coast.

Last October, DailyMail.com spotted the physically fit Kennedy partaking in one of her favorite sports – surfing – in Ventura.
