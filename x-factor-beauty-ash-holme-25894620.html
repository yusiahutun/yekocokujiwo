X Factor beauty Ash Holme is unrecognisable 4 years later in racy lingerie snaps

X Factor star Ash Holme rose to fame on the ITV show when she infamously ditched her band NQ to appear in a new group created by Simon Cowell

The beauty looks totally different now as she persevered with her entertainment career and is still a performer and presenter.

At the time of the show, Ash was a lingerie model and she has stayed true to form with some very scantily-clad snaps on her Instagram page.

In one picture, Ash can be seen posing in a tiny black bra and matching knickers which was covered by a sheer leopard print top and skirt.

Her curves stole the show as she looked off into the distance, showing off her washboard stomach and hourglass hips.

In another snap, Ash smouldered for the camera in a very skimpy black dress which featured a frontless design, with the material only being held together by tiny string.

Ash, who was 21 at the time, was axed from the show by the judges but was brought back to join Simon Cowell's new group, which was called New Girl Band.

The group were put together by Simon after all of the girls were sent home from Boot Camp weeks earlier.

Ash and the rest of her group impressed the judges with their rendition of Hey Mama, but she was left stunned when Dermot O'Leary told her he wanted to bring her back onto the stage.

Ash then performed with New Girl Band after a quick rehearsal and Simon then asked her to ditch her band NQ to come into his new creation.

The group's time on the competition was short-lived as they were knocked out of the Judges' Houses round, with Rak-Sau eventually winning the show.

Simon had hoped to turn them into a successful female version of One Direction, but the girl group didn't take off.
