Whitney Port came to prominence via The Hills in 2006. Post departure from the show she started her reality program, The City, which ran for two seasons. 

She has also appeared in Ireland and Britain's Next Top Model as a judge and hosted online reality game show, Genuine Ken.

Engagement Details

Whitney and her boyfriend, Tim Rosenman got engaged in November 2013. After her fiancé proposed her with an oval stone and rose gold band with three rows of diamonds. She was spotted showing her ring off during the filming of her style web series related to hair products. 

Regarding her engagement, she opened up saying,

I was very surprised! He was planning a whole ordeal -- like possibly taking out a boat and doing this whole thing on the harbor, and then it was like the day before we were leaving and it was really rainy and grey and cold. So everything went down the drainpipe there, So he just did it in our hotel room, he asked me really low key.

Despite the initial plan didn't work out, the later sealed the proposal.

Wedding & Home Info

Former The Hills star married her longtime boyfriend, Tim Rosenman on November 2015 in Palm Springs, California. The couple had a "desert-chic meets Art Deco meets tropical" themed wedding. She walked down the aisle looking more beautiful than ever in a full bridal look with a lace dress. 

The couple then moved to a Venice Beach home by June 2016 after giving it a stylish renovation. Whitney explained,

“We wanted to add a more bohemian, colorful aspect to the décor” 

With a new house and husband, Whitney started her family life.

Mom Life; Baby

Whitney became a mother for the first time when she welcomed her son, Sonny Sanford Rosenman, on 27 July 2017. However, the change took a toll on the reality star as the pregnancy was hard.

Whitney Port shares a moment with husband Tim Rosenman and son Sonny Sanford Rosenman in June 2019 ( Photo: Whitney Port's Instagram)

Further, Sonny was also a difficult child for the first year. Thus, Whitney was scared to become pregnant for the second time.
