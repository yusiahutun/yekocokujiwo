Ebbsfleet United change Yado Mambo's number to five to reference song 'Mambo No 5' by Lou Bega

February 08, 2024
blog

EBBSFLEET UNITED have handed the number five shirt to star Yado Mambo in reference to the song "Mambo No5" by one-hit wonder Lou Bega.

The defender usually wears the number 18 for his team but fans have been demanding a rebrand.

Ebbsfleet listened to their complaints and decided for a one-off treat, the club would make an exclusive shirt with the number 5 on the back and auction it off for charity.

All money goes toward Prostate Cancer UK and fans can bid for the shirt on eBay.

An Ebbsfleet statement read: “Ebbsfleet United hasn’t failed to notice the recent frenzy created by our defender Yado Mambo’s No.5 squad shirt. Or should we say, lack of a No.5 squad shirt.

“It was something we noted six years ago on Yado’s debut but nonetheless, messages have poured in imploring us to swap our squad numbers around so that Mambo and No.5 can be united in perfect musical harmony.
