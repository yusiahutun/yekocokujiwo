Rachel Weisz, 53, rocks leg-lengthening outfit with a twist and she looks gorgeous

March 26, 2024
blog

Rachel Weisz is currently on a whirlwind promotional tour for her new Amazon Prime miniseries, Dead Ringers, and she is slaying her work wardrobe. 

The 53-year-old – who is married to James Bond star, Daniel Craig – made a stop at The Late Show with Stephen Colbert in New York City on Thursday and she looked incredible rocking a leg-lengthening suit. 

Rachel's ensemble was far from boring and accentuated her slender physique. She wore a black suit that was embellished with silver rhinestones and featured a nipped-in waist and low-cut neckline. 

The jacket also boasted structured shoulders and a wide lapel, while the pants flared out at the knees to elongate Rachel's legs. 

She wore her brunette hair down in a center parting with soft waves and added a pop of color with a peach blush and red manicure. 

Rachel's appearance comes after she revealed whether she and her husband will share the big screen together once again. 

The couple – who wed in 2011 – have appeared opposite each other in several films, including Dream House, but Rachel admitted they are in no rush to work together again.

She told Today's Willie Geist: "I think we aren't going to [work together again], at the moment. I think we really love our private life as a life, as a family. And then we go to work separately. 

"We really enjoyed that experience, but also it means we can alternate. So, I can stay home with the family while he works. We can swap out. If we're both doing something at the same time, it's probably less ideal." 

The pair share a five-year-old daughter, whose identity they have kept private, while Rachel has a son from her previous marriage to director Darren Aronofsky, 17-year-old Henry, and Daniel has a 31-year-old daughter, Ella, from his first marriage to actress Fiona Loudon.
