Who Won Rock The Block Season 4 Living Room Off?

May 20, 2024
blog

Team Page Turner and Mitch Glew won Rock the Block 2023 Living Room Face-Off. They are the winners of Season 4 Episode 2.

In Episode 2, the four teams were asked to renovate the living rooms of their identical homes in Berthlad, Colorado. 

Glew and his partner decided to renovate the living area by including a statement fireplace and library.

They planned to have a bathroom for the first-floor bedroom, with a dark tile shower, a stone sink, and a vanity.

They planned on using $30,000 of their limited $250,000 (total) budget to complete their ambitious plan.

Later, when Turner was out to check on the full bathroom, she was surprised after learning they would be completing a half-bathroom. Mitch told, "We just couldn't do the shower. We're out of time."

At the end of week two, Mina Starsiak Hawk from Good Bones and Jenn Todryk from No Demo Reno evaluated the living rooms.

In the end, Mitch and Page's decision to complete a half-bathroom paid off, as the judges loved their indoor library, cozy fireplace, and half-bathroom and crowned them as the winners.

However, many viewers were displeased with the pair's indoor library. The topic even sparked discussions on social media platforms.

One Twitter user wrote, "Disagreed with the Rock the Block winner this week... was not a fan of the floor to ceiling shelves of backward books." 

Another user said he is an avid reader and appreciates the library but not in his living room.

He further added, "great idea bad location?" Page even replied to the tweet and said, "This is a competition so space is limited."
