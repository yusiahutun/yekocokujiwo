World's most admired women - Angelina Jolie tops the list

March 20, 2024
blog

Angelina Jolie remains the most admired woman in the world, according to an annual YouGov survey.

Each year, the polling company conduct a cross-country survey to rank the men and women who are most adored. This year, the survey spanned 35 countries and more than 37,000 people were interviewed by YouGov to compile the list.

Many of the women who made the top 20 are from an entertainment background, with a few from the world of politics and activism included too. The results are calculated from two key questions: whether the respondent admires the figure at all and whether they are the figure they admire the most. The findings from each nation are then weighted per the population size of the country.
