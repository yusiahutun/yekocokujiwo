Christina Loren is a well-recognized meteorologist who currently is serving at the RFD-TV network. Previously, she worked as a meteorologist at NBC Bay Area and CBS Miami.  

Early Life and Education

Christina Loren hails from Palm Desert, California whereas she grew up in the California Bay area. She went to Palm Desert High School. She has a diploma in General Education. She attended California State University Chico and Mississippi State University. She also studied Theology at Grand Canyon University.

Career

Loren began her career working for different stations such as Bakersfield, Chico, and Palm Springs, California. She became a weather forecaster while studying at California State University. Later she joined KHSL and KNTV. She also worked for NBC in, San Francisco, and CBS in Miami. In 2015 she joined RFD as a host of the weather forecast. She was also featured in The Market Day Report show alongside Joe King. She is still an employee at RFD performing her work skillfully.
