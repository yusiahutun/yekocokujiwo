The Missing star Abigail Hardingham goes totally nude in saucy flick

April 18, 2024
blog

The sexy 24-year-old has shot to fame in the role of Alice Webster in the gritty BBC drama.

But fans hoping to see more of her talents should take a peek at the cult comedy-horror flick Nina Forever.

She stripped totally naked for her role as Holly, a paramedic who is haunted in the bedroom by the ghost of her boyfriend’s ex-lover.

The movie, released last year, saw Abigail engage in some seriously steamy scenes as she shared the screen with Cian Barry and Fiona O’Shaughnessy.

Abigail was 21 when the film was shot and the dark comedy involved the three stars spending most of their time nude as they filmed on location in London’s Elephant and Castle area.

She romped in sizzling sex scenes and won the Most Promising Newcomer award at the British Independent Film Awards last year as a result.

Speaking previously about the outlandish role, she said: “There were some really surreal moments.

“But I have to say filming in a graveyard in the middle of the night – and me and Fiona are half-naked – did make me think: ‘What’s going on?’”

It’s a far cry from thriller The Missing, in which her character Alice sparks a manhunt in Germany when she vanishes from a military base.

Abigail has also appeared in the hit TV dramas Broadchurch, Holby City and Waterloo Road.
