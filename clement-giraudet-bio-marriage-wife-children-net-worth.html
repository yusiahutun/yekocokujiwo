Early years of Clement Giraudet

Clement Giraudet was born on June 17, 1984, in Provence, France. Moreover, he hasn’t mentioned the names of his parents. But he is the son of a fashion designer father and dance teacher mother. Besides, he also has one younger sister who is also working as a model.

Additionally, Clement went to Emlyon Business School and received graduation. Later on, he attended the University of Bedfordshire from where he earned a BBA degree in 2007. Even after that, he continued his studies, and then, he enrolled at the EDC Paris Business School. From there, he earned a MBA degree and graduated. Similarly, he also took a major in Luxury Brand Management and International Marketing.

Ever since his childhood, he has a significant curiosity in fashion. Because his father worked, which drove him to pursue a career in the fashion world, Clement began his career as an intern at Christian Dior Couture. After that, he began working at Balmain and served as an assistant store director for three years from 2009 to 2012. Later on, he joined the Saint Laurent company as a store director in 2013.
