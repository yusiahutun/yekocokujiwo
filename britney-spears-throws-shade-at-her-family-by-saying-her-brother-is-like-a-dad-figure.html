Britney Spears 'throws shade' at her family by saying her brother is 'like a dad' figure

August 07, 2024
blog

Britney Spears has taken to her Instagram to share a rare photo of her brother, Bryan Spears, saying he was ‘like a dad’.

The music star has been feuding with her family after she was placed under a 13-year conservatorship. However, it is said that Britney Spears has gotten close to her brother, whilst staying estranged from her mother and father.

Britney Spears says her brother is ‘like a dad’

Taking to her 42.7 million Instagram followers, the Toxic singer shared a photo of her brother, Bryan Spears, in some green overalls and a hard hat.

In the caption, she gushed: “My big brother who’s like a dad and my best friend !! Not sure what’s going on with this outfit though.”

Bryan is Britney’s older sibling. They share a younger sister, Jamie Lee Spears, who has recently left the UK TV show, I’m A Celebrity Get Me Out Of Here!

The two are said to have a strained relationship amid Britney’s conservatorship, and the two even had a public feud over the release of the Zoey 101 alum’s memoir.

However, speaking to her campmates on the reality TV show, currently airing, Jaime said she spoke to her sister shortly before coming in, and “all families fight.”

It was also reported Britney’s big brother stayed over at her mansion to help her out after her split from Sam Asghari. A source told Page Six: “He’s been staying over and helping alongside a therapist.”

The star is now in ‘cordial’ with her ex

Despite what seemed to be a strained relationship at the start of the split, new reports say Britney and Sam are now cordial after a turbulent few months.

Sources told TMZ that the former celebrity couple “is now cordial off the back of their divorce bombshell.”

The insiders also claim that the singer will be signing a “low six-figure check” to her ex to finalize the divorce.
