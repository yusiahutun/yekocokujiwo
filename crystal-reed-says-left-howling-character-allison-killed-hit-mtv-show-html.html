‘I didn’t realise how hard it was going to be!’: Teen Wolf star Crystal Reed says she was left howling after her character Allison was killed off hit MTV show

It was the shock TV death that had Teen Wolf fans around the world howling, and Crystal Reed knows exactly how they feel.

The 29-year-old actress became famous for her breakout role as Allison Argent in the hit MTV series, but in a shock plot twist she was killed off in Monday's dramatic episode.

'Having to take her last breathe... it was really, really difficult,' Reed told the MailOnline.

Tough girl: Crystal Reed as Allison Argent in hit MTV show Teen Wolf

Final moments: Allison's tragic death in the arms of her ex, Scott (Tyler Posey)

'Even though it was my choice to leave the show, I didn't realise how hard it was going to be when it came to filming that final scene.

'So much of her is me and so much of me is her.'

The American actress made her first public appearance since her Teen Wolf death - which came at the end of an enemy's word - at the ASTRA Awards in Sydney on Thursday night.

She stepped out on the red carpet with her boyfriend, The Voice's Darren McMullen, and said she was still 'shocked' by the reactions from fans which have ranged from Tweeting pictures of homemade arrow tattoos - Allison's weapon of choice - to an online memorial.

'I didn’t guess that it would be thought of as an iconic TV death, but that’s been flattering.

Cute couple: Darren McMullen and the American actress on the red carpet at the ASTRA Awards in Sydney on Thursday

'It was actually a really beautiful moment because I felt like something left with me, and I also gave something back.

'I shut a door on my early twenties and opened it up to something much more.'

After four years on the show, Reed told producers she wanted to step away from the series last year so she could explore other TV and film opportunities, and said she has been busy auditioning during US pilot season.

But her grisly end on Teen Wolf came as a massive shock to fans, thanks to the cast and crew remaining tight-lipped about the development.

As a skilled archer and gymnast from a line of werewolf hunters, Allison was a favourite among loyal viewers for being 'much more than a love interest' for pivotal male characters.

Hit series: The cast of supernatural show Teen Wolf on MTV

'She was incredible, a complete female bad a** and there aren't many of those on TV,' said Reed.

'It's getting better, but there aren't.

'The thing that I love about her is she’s a bad a** and she was honest and truthful, but at the same time she never forgot her femininity.

'She could look deadly with a bow and arrow, but she also wore lip gloss.

'Because a lot times I feel filmmakers want to take away their womanhood and make them masculine, but she didn’t lose that.

'I'm really honoured to have played her.'
