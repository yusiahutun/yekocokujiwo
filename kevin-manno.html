Kevin Manno is a American television and radio host known for his work in shows such as 'Abby's Ultimate Dance Competition' and 'The Seven.' He also remains in the news because of his relationship with former 'Bachelorette' Ali Fedotowsky. 

Kevin Manno's Wife is 'The Bachelorette' Famed Television Personality Ali Fedotowsky

Kevin Manno is happily married to entertainment journalist and former The Bachelorette star Ali Fedotowsky. Fedotowsky appeared in the season six of the dating reality show and got engaged to Roberto Martinez. Sadly the relationship did not last long, and the couple broke up in November 2011.

Though The Bachelorette did not help her find her one true love, it surely started a path to meet her husband. After appearing in the reality show, she left her job at Facebook and became a TV Personality. In the beginning, she joined the local Fox news station as a weekly morning show correspondent, and later she hosted the weekly lifestyle television show 1st Look. 

Kevin Manno got engaged with his long time girlfriend, Ali Fedotowsky, in 2015. SOURCE: NY Daily News

While working in the NBC Show in 2013, Fedotowsky was introduced to Kevin by the producer of the show. Soon after the first meeting, Manno and Fedotowsky started dating, and two years later, in September 2015 got engaged.

The couple initially wanted to get married on June 2016 in Mexico; however, it was postponed because of Zika virus concerns. Hence, Manno and Ali tied the Knot on March 3, 2017, in Los Angeles. The wedding was attended by celebrities such as Haylie Duff, Kaley Cuoco, Lacey Chabert, and Andi Dorfman in addition to friends and family.

Kevin Manno with wife Ali Fedotowsky and their two children. SOURCE: Reality TV World

Three years has passed since the wedding, and the couple is now parents to two children; daughter Molly Sullivan Manno and son Riley Doran Manno. 

Kevin Manno's First Marriage ended in Divorce

Kevin was married to a woman named Kris Mathes from 2011-2012. The reason for their split is not known. Kevin do not have any kids from his first marriage. 

Kevin Manno's Net Worth

Kevin started his career as a radio host. He has worked at several stations, including Q101. Later he transitioned into television hosting and, as of now, has hosted many popular programs such as Abby's Ultimate Dance Competition, and The Seven.

Currently, he hosts the program Valentine In The Morning at 1043MYFM. His Net Worth as of 2020 is estimated to be around $1.5 million.
