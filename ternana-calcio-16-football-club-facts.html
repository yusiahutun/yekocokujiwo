Ternana Calcio Home Stadium

Ternana Calcio plays their home matches at the Stadio Libero Liberati, which can accommodate over 22,000 spectators. The stadium, named after a former president of the club, creates an electric atmosphere on match days, with fans filling the stands and cheering on their beloved team.
