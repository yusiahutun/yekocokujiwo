Taylor Swift has ‘reopened’ her feud with Kim Kardashian, after dropping the single thanK you aIMee, and it looks like Meghan Markle may have chosen a side…

Anyone with an Instagram account would have seen Meghan Markle‘s jam being promoted for her lifestyle brand, American Riviera Orchard, and one of these lucky receivers happened to be Kris Jenner. Taylor Swift may not be promoting it any time soon, then…

The Duchess of Sussex sent out 50 jars of her strawberry jam to celebrities and influencers, each with their own unique number. Kris Jenner received (unlucky for some) number 13, as she took to her Instagram stories to thank Meghan.

“Thank you @americanriveriaorchard,” Kris wrote alongside a pic of the product.

“She’s picked the wrong side in the Taylor Swift feud scenario,” Talk TV’s Mike Graham said. “If she’s siding herself with the Kardashians, doesn’t that mean that the Taylor Swifities will come for her? This is one of the enemy.”

Royal expert Kinsey Schofield happened to agree, as she joked: “There’s no way in h*** I would go against the Swifties.”

She then went on to speak about the time Meghan reportedly ‘handwrote a note’ to Taylor, asking her to come on her Archetypes podcast.

However, the Bad Blood singer’s representatives reportedly declined, instead of the singer responding directly.

Recently, it was reported Kris Jenner wanted Prince Harry and Meghan to cameo in the hit show, and the couple reportedly saw “no harm” in doing so.

The Duchess will not join Prince Harry on his trip to England next month

Prince Harry will be returning to England next month for the tenth anniversary of the Invictus Games. His visit has been planned for a while, and there have been months of speculation whether his wife would be joining or not.

It’s now been revealed she will not be joining the Duke on the trip back to his home country, as royal expert Chris Ship took to X, formerly known as Twitter, to confirm the news.

“Additional confirmation this morning that Meghan will not be joining Harry for the Invictus Anniversary service at @StPaulsLondon in London,” he wrote. “But the Duchess of Sussex will join the Duke for an Invictus-inspired visit to Nigeria immediately after Harry leaves the UK.”

The mom of two was last in the UK in September 2022, as she was due to appear at the WellChild Awards. However, they had to pull out due to the death of Queen Elizabeth II and ended up staying in the UK longer than originally planned.
