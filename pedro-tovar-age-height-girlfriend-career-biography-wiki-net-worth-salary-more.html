Early Life

Pedro Tavor was born on July 2 in 2002 in California, USA. He is 20 years old.

From an early age along with his brother Brian, he is trained in singing and playing various musical instruments including guitars, bass guitars, and drums.

Physical Appearance

Pedro stands 5’5″ in height and his weight is around 60 kg. His eyes are dark brown  and hair is black in color. He has adopted a funky cool look and he has also a piercing on his right ear.

Career

Pedro is gaining a lot of recognition lately. He is the main vocalist of his band “Eslabon Armado” which he formed with his brother and friends in 2017. The unique style of their band in producing music and catering it, makes them special and is loved by many. Pedro started his career at a young age.

After the release of “Vibras De Noche” he debuted at No.1 on Billboard Latin Songwriters Chart. And he was also awarded with Billboard Music Award for Top Duo/ Latin Group in 2021. He is followed massively on social media platforms like TikTok and Instagram. He is famous for his lip syncs and musical shorts on TikTok. He is even has a good number of listeners on musical platform Spotify.
