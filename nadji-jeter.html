Nadji Jeter is a famous actor, dancer, composer, and musician famous for his roles in “Reed Between the Lines”, and “The Grown”. Nadji Jeter got a nomination for the BAFTA Award for Performer in a Leading Role in the 2020 video game, “Spider-Man: Miles Morales”.

Early Life and Childhood

The young actor’s full birth name is Nadji Anthony Jeter. His birthdate is 18th October 1996 and his birthplace is in Atlanta, Georgia, United States. Similarly, his current age is twenty-seven years whereas his zodiac sign is Libra.

The actor’s nationality is America and he is of African-American ethnicity who has been following the Christian religion. However, the actor seems to prefer to keep the information about his family and childhood private. Still, some of the details about his earlier life ultimately come in front of everyone.

The actor was always interested in dancing, acting, and comedy acting from a very young age. These amazing talents were found by his parents and they even supported him to grow in a similar field.

At the age of nine years, he gave a dance performance at Usher’s New Look Foundation Gala. He even performed as a dancing mascot for the Atlanta Hawks recognized as Lil Harry the Hawk of the NBA.

As for his education, he initially went to a local school in Alabama. Then, he went to a middle school in Los Angeles, graduating in 2014, and eventually started homeschooling.

Career and Professional Life

Nadji’s professional acting career took off from the year 2006 and still is running very well. The young actor has had several chances to be a part of several TV series and movies to this day. His first movie work was “Dirty Laundry” in the year 2006.

In the same year, he got the role of a performer in the film, Beats Style and Flavor”. Next, he got the role of Bobby in the episode: “A Change Is Gonna Come” of the 2007 live-action film, “Grey’s Anatomy”. Then, he went to Los Angeles in the same year to chase his acting career.

The actor also was present in two episodes of the TV drama, “Everybody Hates Chris” as the guy, Jason. He got a Sling Shot Kid character in the movie, “Lower Learning” in the same year.

He also showed his appearance as Jasper in the film, “Opposite Day” and as the Football Kid #2 in the episode: “Prisoner Jane” in “The Forgotten”. Likewise, he got a chance to act as Andre McKenzie in the movies, “Grown Ups” (2010), and “Grown Ups 2” (2013).

The American actor also got the character of Keenan Reynolds in the 2011 sitcom, “Reed Between the Lines”. Additionally, he acted as Joey Malone in the episode: “Under the Influence” of “Castle” in 2013.

In 2015, the American actor was present in the episode: “Basket Cases” in “Jessie” as Terry. He also got the role of Brandon Larabee to act in the episode: “Vanessa Fixes Up Eve” in “Last Man Standing” in the same year. The artist acted as Hunter in “Dance Camp” and as Poundcake in “The Fifth Wave”, both in the year 2016.

Some more

In 2017, he got the role of Justin in the film, “Wonder” and of Nick Gordon in the TV film, “Bobbi Kristina”. He got the voice character of Miles Morales (Ultimate Spider-Man) in the film, “Spider-Man” from 2017 to 2020.

Moreover, the American actor was present as Blake in the 2021 movie, “The Runner”. Adding more to his profession, he has also worked as voice and motion characters in several video games.

Jeter made his debut in this field as the voice of Sam in the action-adventure video game, “The Last of Us” in 2013. Likewise, he worked as Miles Morales in the 2018 video game, “Marvel’s Spider-Man”.

In 2019, the voice actor got the repetitive role of Miles Morales (Spider-Man) in “Marvel Ultimate Alliance 3: The Black Order”. With the same character, he worked in 2020’s “Marvel’s Spider-Man: Miles Morales”, and 2023’s “Marvel’s Spider-Man 2”.

Then, he was present in several national commercials and even was the face of Coca-Cola in 2011. The actor later showed his appearance in a YouTube Red original film, “Dance Camp”.

Furthermore, Nadji is a Star Power Representative of the Starlight Children’s Foundation. He has also been tangled in Usher’s New Look Foundation from the early age of 6. He was honored with the 2013 Global Youth Leadership Award for his contribution to the foundation.
