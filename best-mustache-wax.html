The tail end of “Movember” or “No Shave November” looms, so it’s as good a time as any to think about what comes next. If you’ve spent the past month curating new facial hair or a mustache, and if you think it looks good (bonus points if your partner agrees), then caring for it has got to be a crucial next step. Even if you’re a seasoned facial hair vet and have been sporting a ‘stache long before Ryan Gosling rocked one in The Nice Guys, a refresher helps. This is where one of the very best mustache waxes goes a long way.

Everything to Know About the Best Mustache Waxes

But first, what is mustache wax? And why do you need it? The best mustache waxes are part of a growing subset of men’s grooming categories, like the best ball deodorants, that take an overlooked category and get ultra-specific (and high-quality). Mustache wax is nearly exactly what it sounds like: A balmy wax that can be worked onto your fingers and then worked down to the root of your mustache to provide a styling hold and moisture (not unlike beard balm, of course). Some also have a consistency closer to that of a cream or a beard butter. 

You’ll find it delivered most commonly in small circular tins, which makes for easy transport and beard care on the go. Common ingredients include beeswax and shea butter for moisture, among others. And like a pomade, you’ll see various beard and mustache wax and balm products labeled according to the amount of “hold” they provide your facial hair. 

“Whether you’re going for a laid-back vibe or a more sculpted look, the right hold is key. But remember, comfort is king. A wax that’s too stiff or sticky can be a real downer,” says Faiysal Kothiwala, Chief Bearded Officer of men’s beard care brand The Beard Struggle. Those moisturizing elements are vital, Kothiwala says. “Conditioning is not just for the hair on your head. A good wax can keep your mustache soft and healthy.” Kothiwala adds that well-groomed facial hair, like a well-tailored suit, simply begets more confidence. 

The Return of the ‘Stache (& What It Means For You)

It’s more important than ever to consider adding a mustache wax to your medicine cabinet, as the facial hair style itself comes back into vogue, Kothiwala notes. “Fashion is a spinning wheel, and the mustache is having its moment once more. It’s a nod to the past with a modern twist,” he says. 

The style has made a comeback thanks in no small part to what Kothiwala calls “famous faces” that tend to turn heads. “That kind of visibility can really set a trend,” he adds, noting that the pandemic inspired many to experiment with facial hair trends and styles. 

Harkening back to “Movember,” Kothiwala notes that the mustache stands for something beyond just leading man-inspired style, calling it with “grooming with a cause.” A few key pillars should guide the application and continued use of your mustache wax, Kothiwala adds, including proper scent (since it’s right under your nose) and natural ingredients, which are gentle on the skin. “If you’re struggling to apply the wax, that’s a no-go. The ideal wax should be a breeze to work into your mustache,” Kothiwala says.
