Growing up in the limelight, Delilah has managed to step out of her parents’ shadow, proving she’s not just another nepo baby. Now, at 25, she’s already made the leap from Hollywood royalty to fashion icon. Here are some must-know facts about her.

Delilah Belle Hamlin launched a clothing brand with her sister

Three years apart in age, the sisters appear close and are frequently seen out together in Los Angeles.

In 2019, they released an athleisure line called DNA in partnership with LA Collective. But although they occasionally collaborate on photo shoots and campaigns, Delilah and Amelia are each building their own, independent careers in the modelling world.

From reality TV to the runway

Delilah’s modelling journey began unexpectedly in her backyard, with sister Amelia as her photographer for casual Instagram shoots. These sessions caught the attention of Elite Model Management, kick-starting her ascent in the fashion world. Delilah told WWD, “I never planned on modelling. It was just a fun journey that began with an Instagram post.”

The award goes to … A-listers rocking archival fashion on red carpets

In 2017, Delilah’s career reached new heights when she signed with IMG Models, leading to her runway debut at New York Fashion Week for Tommy Hilfiger, and appearances for brands like Alice + Olivia and Dolce & Gabbana. She’s also been featured in publications such as Elle, Harper’s Bazaar, Teen Vogue and Vogue Japan.

She studied criminal psychology at NYU

After graduating from high school, Delilah made a beeline for New York City. By January 2018, she was not only walking runways but also studying criminal psychology at NYU. “I have no idea how I’m going to balance modelling and school, but my agency knows I’m still a student,” she told Teen Vogue back in 2016.

She has also dived into music and acting

Besides modelling, Delilah has dipped her toe in the music and film industries. March 2023 saw the release of her debut single and music video, “Nothing Lasts Forever”. Delilah also made her film debut in 2023, in the thriller How She Caught a Killer, in which she plays a former sex worker who aspires to become a nurse.

But her acting journey didn’t end there. Delilah and mum Rinna have their first on-screen collaboration in the form of Mommy Meanest, a Lifetime movie set to premiere later this year – inspired by true events about a teenager suffering from cyberbullying, who shockingly discovers that the culprit is her own mother.

She’s in a committed relationship

Delilah is currently in a relationship with actor Henry Eikenberry. The couple officially stepped into the limelight together during the red carpet event for the premiere of Eikenberry’s Apple TV+ series The Crowded Room earlier this month.

Eikenberry shared with People that his relationship with Delilah seems to have received her parents’ seal of approval. He added jokingly that Delilah’s dad has already taken him aside for a few heart-to-hearts.
