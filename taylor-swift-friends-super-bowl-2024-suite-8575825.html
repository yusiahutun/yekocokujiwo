Who Is Taylor Swift Sitting With at the Super Bowl?

March 22, 2024
blog

Besides the football of it all, Taylor Swift may be the biggest draw of 2024's Super Bowl LVIII. The will-she-won't-she speculation about whether she'd make it to Las Vegas in time for the game after back-to-back Eras Tour shows in Tokyo had the buzz around the NFL championship at an all-time high.

So, when she showed up to Allegiant Stadium to cheer on BF Travis Kelce and the Kansas City Chiefs as they take on the San Francisco 49ers alongside Blake Lively and Ice Spice, it was clear she was bringing a host of friends and family with her to the Big Game. So, who exactly was in Taylor Swift's suite at Super Bowl LVIII? Read on for everyone she hosted.

Blake Lively

Swift's close friend and long-time Girls' Night Out bestie, Blake Lively joined Taylor Swift in her box. (In a Chiefs red track suit, no less.) She's also attended Kansas City Chiefs games with Swift in the past, including when the team faced off against the New York Jets in October.

Ice Spice

Ice Spice just collaborated with Swift on her “Karma” remix on the Midnights album, so it stands to reason the two are close. The "Boys a Liar" rapper, who showed up to the game in a black zip up and her signature red curls, has also been nominated for awards alongside Swift in the past.

Ashley Avignone

One of Swift’s longtime friends is stylist and designer Ashley Avignone. She and Swift met through Emma Stone, according to People, and Avignone cheered on KC last year when they played the New England Patriots.

Lana Del Rey

Close friend and fellow singer Lana Del Rey joined Swift midway through the game to chat and hang out after standing in the box in front of Swift earlier.

Miles Teller and Keleigh Sperry

Miles Teller and his wife Keleigh Sperry, who got married in 2019, also joined the Midnights singer for the Super Bowl.

RIOTUSA

Ice Spice brought her music producer RIOTUSA to the Super Bowl (he's wearing a black t-shirt and standing to the left of Ice Spice above). He was first spotted walking ahead of Swift in a video of her entering the stadium earlier this evening.

Andrea Swift

Swift's mom, Andrea, who's been supportive of her and Kelce's relationship since the beginning, also attended the game. She wore a red scarf, black t-shirt, and ponytail to the event.

Scott Swift

Taylor's dad, Scott Swift, attended the Super Bowl with his daughter, wife, and son, Austin. He wore a red shirt and stood behind his wife, Andrea, in the box.

Austin Swift

Austin Swift, Taylor's younger brother, attended the Super Bowl with his family. He notably also went with his sister to the Chiefs' Christmas Day game and got into the festive mood wearing a Santa Suit.

Jason Kelce

Travis Kelce's brother and fellow NFL player, who plays for the Philadelphia Eagles, showed up to support his brother and joined Taylor Swift and his family to watch the game together.

Kylie Kelce

Kylie Kelce, Travis Kelce’s sister-in-law, joined the Swift family and friends to watch the game alongside her husband and mother-in-law.

Donna Kelce

Donna Kelce, Travis and Jason's mom, who has said she's never seen her son so happy as during his time dating Swift, joined the singer to watch the Super Bowl and cheer on her son.
