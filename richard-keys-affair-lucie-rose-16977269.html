Love rat and 'pathological liar' Richard Keys caught again with pretty blonde lawyer

April 02, 2024
blog

The disgraced former Sky Sports host begged his wife of 34 years to take him back after his romance with 28-year-old Lucie Rose was discovered last year.

Keys and Lucie spent two nights together in a £400-a-night five star hotel in London this week, MailOnline reports.

Long-suffering wife Julia has branded her husband a "pathological liar".

She is understood to be filing for divorce from her 59-year-old husband.

Julia said: "When someone, after 34 years of marriage, can do this to his wife and still plead for her to take him back, it really does beggar belief as to who this man is because I don't know him.

"I really don't know who he is.

"He promised me that he would not see Lucie again.

"And this week he sent me texts saying he was categorically not with her and had no feelings for her any more.

"He wrote: 'Please let me come home, I love you, I can't live without you'. He is a pathological liar."

Lucie met Keys through his 31-year-old daughter Jemma in Doha, Qatar, where he is the face of the BeIN sports channels.

After news of the affair broke last year, Julia, 56, completed a dramatic U-turn – calling off the divorce 24 hours after she announced they were splitting.

The couple have two children together – Jemma and 27-year-old Josh.
