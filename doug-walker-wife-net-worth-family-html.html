Doug Walker, mostly known with his online name “That Guy With The Glasses," is a YouTube star, film reviewer, critic, comedian, and actor.

Doug rose to stardom when he created a web-series titled, Nostalgia Critic on YouTube along with his brother. In the show, he acts in a character of “Nostalgia Critic" by criticizing movies and presenting an analysis of the subject of the story with his justifications.

 Also, his web series also showcases many “Top 11 Countdown” segments and features a series of Old vs. New sections where Doug compares popular films like Batman, Cinderella, Chocolate Factory, True Grit, and Spider-Man with their new remakes.

Moreover, Doug is also the founder of the online production company, Channel Awesome.
