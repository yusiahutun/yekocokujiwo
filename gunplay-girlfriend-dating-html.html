American rapper Gunplay has had a successful musical career. Following the rapper's solo debut on a 2009 Rick Ross track entitled Gunplay, he released his debut studio album, Living Legend, in 2015. Some of his famous songs include Power Circle (2012), Cartoon & Cereal (2013), and Finals 2 (2014). 

Dating Life

The Texas native was dating an Instagram star- Keyara Stone.

In 2019, the news of their break up surfaced as the rapper allegedly cheated on girlfriend Keyara with Amara La Negra. In one of the promo clips of Love & Hip Hop: Miami, Gunplay stated that Amara sliding into his DMs contributed to his fallout with Keyara. He further added that he hoped to marry Keyara one day.

Failed Marriage And Children Details

Gunplay was married to a Bahamian woman named Phillippa Tanya McCartney in 2004. But, the pair divorced after four years. They have a son named Richard Morales, and they share his custody.

Criminal Offences

While the rapper was dating Keyara, he got arrested for head-butting his ex-girlfriend Aneka Johnston.

The reported altercation took place on New Year's Day of 2018 at The Office Strip Club in Miami. Gunplay was there with his then-girlfriend Keyara while Aneka was to dance at the night club. Reportedly, he and Aneka got involved in verbal arguments. And following that, Gunplay attacked her with his head. 

Aneka had bruises on her face and neck, as well as a scratched cornea. She pressed criminal charges and a charge for misdemeanor battery against the rapper. Also, she accused him of being violent during their relationship between 2010-2012.
