The Secret to Blurred, Smooth-Looking Skin Is Armanis Tinted Moisturizer

March 25, 2024
blog

I am by no means a professional makeup artist, but when I do get my look just right, I don’t want it flaking off within hours thanks to dry skin season, amirite? That’s why I’ve been searching for a tinted moisturizer that can give me a layer of hydration with light coverage that my dull complexion needs. And I think I found just that with the Armani Neo Nude True-to-Skin Natural Glow Foundation, that’s 30 percent off at Nordstrom, so I’m adding it to my cart.

The tinted moisturizer, from a brand used by Sydney Sweeney and Maude Apatow for its full foundation, combines pigments to enhance your skin tone with hydrating ingredients for an overall healthy glow. Hyaluronic acid increases moisture in the skin to provide a smooth, plump appearance, while glycerin pulls in even more water to deeper layers and helps to seal it in. Over time, this will help to reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.

Armani Neo Nude True-to-Skin Natural Glow Foundation

The Armani tinted moisturizer gives you the freedom to use it as a dewy base for additional makeup products, or you can wear it solo for light, natural coverage over the entire face. Plus, you can customize your coverage by either applying a small amount for a subtle look, or add another layer on top for a more deeply hydrating, pigmented appearance. Applying the tinted moisturizer is easy. Start with a small amount, and spread it over your entire face with either your fingertips or a foundation brush. Continue to blend it from the center of your face outward in gentle strokes until it’s been evenly distributed.  

The natural glow foundation has earned rave reviews from shoppers, including those who have officially ditched their typical foundation in place of this tinted moisturizer. One reviewer who started using it “two years ago” said that it’s “very lightweight and provides great coverage.” They noted that they “look very natural” with it on, and even though they have “very oily skin,” it “remains intact all day with no shine.” A 70-year-old customer said that it’s “great for older dry skin,” noting that it “helps even out [their] skin but looks [like] no makeup.”

Shoppers also like that it offers buildable coverage. One person said that “it leaves a beautiful satin finish,” adding that it “makes your skin look like it’s been blurred and smoothed by a real-life photo filter.” After applying, the tinted moisturizer “lasted all day” without “creasing, moving, or smudging.”
