As Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift’s relationship continues to take over the world, more resurfaced videos of the NFL star are starting to come out, and the latest is him playing a game of Kiss, Marry, Kill, choosing between Katy Perry, Taylor, and Ariana Grande. However, his response may shock you.

The celebrity couple went public in September after Taylor Swift was spotted cheering on the NFL star at a Kansas City Chiefs game. Travis Kelce had a crush on the singer before they got together, even trying to get a friendship bracelet to her backstage on her Eras Tour. However, according to this unearthed video of Travis Kelce playing Kiss, Marry, or Kill, he may have had a bigger crush on Katy Perry years ago….

Travis Kelce plays ‘Kiss, Marry, Kill’ – but he doesn’t pick Taylor Swift

In 2016, the Kansas City Chiefs Tight End was asked to participate in a game of Kiss, Marry, Kill in an interview, and the ‘culprits’ Travis Kelce was met with, were Taylor Swift, Katy Perry, and Ariana Grande.

“Damn, that’s messed up!” the star responded. “I don’t want to kill any of them.”

As the interviewer laughed and told him it was just a game, he responded, “Ariana, I’d kill, unfortunately. Love you, but you’re gone.”

With his ‘so 2016’ quiff haircut, Trav then went on to say he’d kiss now girlfriend Taylor, and marry Katy Perry! Well, he definitely manifested kissing Taylor, as we’ve seen in the many pics and videos!

His answer screams Bad Blood

This is even more awkward considering the Bad Blood – pardon the pun – between Taylor and Katy. Perry confirmed the frenemies on and off friendship with the Lover hitmaker with James Corden in 2017. It appears they made up but the years-long feud is as spicy as her song lyrics. I wonder what Tay Tay thought if she ever saw the clip of Travis Kelce choosing Katy in Kiss, Marry or Kill.

Although they haven’t even been together for a year, Swiftie’s are convinced Travis is ‘the one’ for her, as it seems so different from her past relationships.

Taylor’s last public relationship with Joe Alwyn was notoriously private, despite it lasting for six years. We’ve had more Trav content in six months than Joe in six years!

Celebrity psychic Adam Norton also told Celebrity Tidbit that he predicts 2024 is the year Kelce will pop the question, so watch this space!
