Eternal flame! Red Robin brings back fan favorite Burnin' Love Burger

April 11, 2024
blog

Red Robin has brought back its Burnin' Love burger - and fans are thrilled that their cherished sandwich is back.

The popular burger is layered with fried jalapeño coins, homemade salsa, pepper-jack, lettuce, tomato and chipotle aioli - topped with a brioche bun and served with a side of the restaurant's signature steak fries.

The company claims the burger is 'bigger and juicier' than ever. 

The new offering  is priced around $15.99.

The popular burger is layered with fried jalapeño coins, homemade salsa, pepper-jack, lettuce, tomato and chipotle aioli and is topped with a brioche bun

The Burnin' Love burger will be available nationwide

It is unclear when the restaurant, headquartered in Colorado, removed the item from the menu, but the move disappointed many patrons.

Some even admitted that the Burnin' Love was the only reason they were such a loyal customer and frequented the food chain, while others claimed it as their 'favorite burger of all time.' 

Some contended that the restaurant's other signature burgers, the Mad Love Burger and the Scorpion burger, 'didn't come close to Burnin' Love'. 

One foodie even tried to transform their Scorpion burger into the Burnin' Love by  taking a screenshot of the ingredients listed on the company's website and then replicating it.

'I was SO mad when I found out they got rid of it,' wrote one fan of the burger on Redditt. 

'Literally the only thing I've ever gotten at Red Robin for years and my favorite burger of all time. They told me they were replacing it with the scorpion burger (which I tried and is not as good).

'The ingredients are listed on the website still so I screenshoted it and intend to just modify the scorpion to recreate it.'

But when word got out that the burger may be making a comeback, the conversation went viral. 

One customer took to Redditt last month to share that they wrote to customer service to share how much they missed Burnin' Love.  

'The Burnin' Love burger was the only burger my family ordered at Red Robin. We were disappointed that it was taken out from the menu. We let the customer service team know that we would love Red Robin to bring this burger back,' they wrote.

'They let us know that we were not the only ones that made this request. So, I would suggest you all to let the managers/customer service reps know that you would like to see this burger back on the menu.'
