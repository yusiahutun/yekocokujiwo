World's largest tequila bar launches bingo bottomless brunch with 'non-stop fun'

May 17, 2024
blog

Are you planning a bottomless brunch session with all your pals this Christmas?

Well if you fancy the drinks along with a game of bingo, you can head to the world's largest tequila bar.

Located in the heart of London is Barrio Covent Garden which promises non-stop entertainment to customers.

After opening as the world's largest tequila bar in October, the joint launched its La Bamba Bingo Brunch this month.

Barrio is well known for its unique bottomless brunch concepts across other sites.

And its latest offering will be the craziest one yet as there's a twist on a game of bingo with a whole party element.

Expect dance-offs, rave rounds, catwalk vogue-offs plus incredible prizes in all of Barrio's usual day party madness.

With the host La Bamba driving the non-stop entertainment, the new event will lead to an unforgettable night out.

It promises an evening of silly games from Hook-a-Duck, Space Hopper races to blindfolded fruit machines challenges.

And don't forget, there's bottomless brunch involved too.

Other surprises include Barrio's all-star DJs with a mix of hits from across the ages.

Every Saturday from 1pm until 4:30pm, guests will be able to let loose while enjoying bottomless drinks.

From £39-£50 per person, visitors can expect a 2-course menu of beef, chicken, halloumi or vegan burgers.

Churros and chocolate sauce comes for dessert with an endless supply of Prosecco, sangria and Sol beer for two hours.

Don't worry if alcohol isn't your thing as you can also enjoy mocktail options too.

Jim Robertson, Managing Director of Barrio Familia, said: "This will be our biggest Barrio to date.

"We believe Barrio to be the biggest tequila bar in the world.

"It's going to be an incredible launch and we couldn't be more ready for this next fiesta!"

For tickets or more information, visit the Design my Night website.
